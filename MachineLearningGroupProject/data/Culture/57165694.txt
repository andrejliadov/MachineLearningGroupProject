A '''cultural manager''' ({{lang-es|'''gestor cultural'''}}) is a person who is motivated by the improvement of [[art]], works independently and professionally with knowledge of the subject, and develops work as a mediator between governmental and/or private cultural institutions with artists from different areas to articulate their work in the market with promotion and national and international dissemination.<ref name=uoc>{{Cite web |url=http://gestiocultural.blogs.uoc.edu/2016/06/28/que-es-un-gestor-cultural-gestion-cultural/ |title=¿Qué es un gestor cultural? |trans-title=What is a Cultural Manager? |publisher=[[Open University of Catalonia]] |language=Spanish |date=28 June 2016 |access-date=18 April 2018}}</ref>

The cultural manager works by looking for specific measures of success, always intending to improve the level of culture, seeking active cohesion between society, the governmental sector, the private sector, and the artists. The work of culture management poses learning challenges in diverse areas such as the administration of economic resources, training, and artistic communication.<ref name=uoc/><ref>{{Cite web |url=https://www.educaweb.com/profesion/gestor-cultural-334/ |title=Gestor cultural |publisher=Educaweb |language=Spanish |access-date=18 April 2018}}</ref>

Cultural management is a new profession. A person who is dedicated to this work is characterized by having abilities to visualize and interpret talent, knowing how to establish a dialogue with artists to link them to cultural projects to develop.<ref>{{Cite book |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2hHxeCA_ecYC&pg=PA13 |title=Gestión cultural: Entre conceptos lejanos y realidades cercanas |trans-title=Cultural Management: Between Distant Concepts and Nearby Realities |chapter=Acercando realidades a través de la gestión cultural |trans-chapter=Bringing Together Realities Through Cultural Management |first1=Silvia |last1=Aballay |first2=Carla Fernanda |last2=Avendaño Manelli |publisher=Eduvim |isbn=9789871518968 |pages=13–16 |language=Spanish |year=2010 |access-date=18 April 2018 |via=Google Books}}</ref>
