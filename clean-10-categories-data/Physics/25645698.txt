'''''Physics''''' is an [[open access]] online publication containing commentaries on the best of the [[peer-reviewed]] research published in the journals of the [[American Physical Society]]. The [[editor-in-chief]] of ''Physics'' is Jessica Thomas.<ref name="about">[http://physics.aps.org/about About ''Physics'']</ref> It highlights papers in ''[[Physical Review Letters]]'' and the ''[[Physical Review]]'' family of journals.<ref name="about"/> The magazine was established in 2008.<ref name="announce_physics">[http://publish.aps.org/edannounce/PhysRevLett.101.120001 APS Announces ''Physics'']</ref>

==Features==
''Physics'' contains three types of commentaries on research papers: journalistic articles ("Focus"), in depth pieces written by active researchers ("Viewpoints"), and short summaries of a research paper ("Synopsis") written by editorial staff.<ref name="about"/> Readers get free access to the underlying research papers on which the commentaries are based.
