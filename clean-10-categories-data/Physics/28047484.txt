The '''Alfredo di Braccio Award''' is a prestigious prize for young [[Italy|Italian]] scientists given by the Italian [[Accademia Nazionale dei Lincei]]. <ref> {{cite web|url=https://www.lincei.it/it/content/premio-%E2%80%9Calfredo-di-braccio%E2%80%9D-studiosi-fisica-0|title=Premio “Alfredo Di Braccio” per studiosi in Fisica|publisher=L’Accademia Nazionale dei Lincei|accessdate= 4 September 2020}} </ref>

==Award winners==

Every year a top young chemist or physicist receives this honor for his research.

* '''2008''' ''Chemistry'' prize was awarded to Dr. [https://web.archive.org/web/20110722063715/http://chifis.unipv.it/malavasi/topic/index.html Lorenzo Malavasi] (University of Pavia, Italy).
* '''2009''' ''Physics'' prize was awarded (ex aequo) to Dr. Alessandro Mirizzi (National Institute of Nuclear Physics, Italy) and Dr. [http://bec.science.unitn.it/infm-bec/people/recati.html Alessio Recati] (CNR researcher at the University of Trento, Italy).
* '''2010''' ''Chemistry'' prize was awarded to Dr. [https://web.archive.org/web/20130712151909/http://barongroup.medchem.utah.edu/ Riccardo Baron] (University of Utah, U.S.A.).
* '''2011''' ''Physics'' prize was awarded (ex aequo) to Dr. [http://www.epsyoungminds.org/member/antonio.politano/ Antonio Politano] (post-doc at Università degli Studi della Calabria, Italy) and Dr. Alessandro Giuliani (Researcher at Università di Roma Tre, Italy)
* '''2012''' ''Chemistry'' prize was awarded to Tiziano Montini
* '''2013''' ''Physics'' prize was awarded to Francesco Pellegrino and Pasquale Serpico
* '''2014''' ''Physics'' prize was awarded to Stefano Protti
* '''2015''' ''Physics'' prize was awarded to Michele Cicoli
* '''2016''' ''Chemistry'' prize was awarded to Francesca Maria Toma
* '''2017''' ''Physics'' prize was awarded to Marco Genoni
* '''2019''' ''Physics'' prize awarded to Matteo Lucchini and Andrea Crespi  <ref> https://www.capable-erc.eu/andrea-crespi-awarded-with-the-alfredo-di-braccio-prize/</ref><ref>https://www.fisi.polimi.it/en/home/news/57177</ref>
