A '''Kneipp facility''' is a construction or devise in a [[spa]] or special park that is used for the Kneipp hydrotherapy, also called "Kneipp Cure" or "Kneippsim".<ref>{{Citation|title=Kneippism|url=https://www.thefreedictionary.com/kneippism|work=The Free Dictionary|access-date=2020-09-14}}</ref> It was founded and developed by the Bavarian-German priest [[Sebastian Kneipp|Sebastion Kneipp]] in the 19th century who was one of the forefathers of the [[Naturopathy|naturopathic]] medicine movement.

The Kneipp facilities are mostly water oriented applications which have various methods, temperatures and pressure, for example water treading facilities and arm basins fed by springs, water pumps or the public drinking water network.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Kneipp cure|url=https://medical-dictionary.thefreedictionary.com/Kneipp+cure|access-date=2020-09-14|website=TheFreeDictionary.com}}</ref> It also includes constructions like barefoot walking paths and variations for children.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Bauhinweise Kneipp-Anlage {{!}} Kneipp-Bund|url=https://www.kneippbund.de/download-center/bauhinweise-kneipp-anlage/|access-date=2020-09-14|website=www.kneippbund.de|language=de}}</ref> It is especially common in the German speaking countries<ref>{{Cite web|last=Tourismus|first=Schweiz|title=Kneipp facilities|url=https://www.myswitzerland.com/en-us/experiences/kneipp-facilities/|access-date=2020-09-14|website=Switzerland Tourism|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Kneipp treatments {{!}} Wellnessurlaub in Bavaria|url=https://www.bavaria.by/experiences/spa-breaks-bavaria/wellness-glossary/kneipp-kur/|access-date=2020-09-14|website=Bayern Tourismus|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Kneipp garden|url=https://www.upperaustria.com/en/oesterreich-poi/detail/430004102/kneipp-garden.html|access-date=2020-09-14|website=www.upperaustria.com|language=en}}</ref> and nowadays also part of spa areas in international luxury hotels.<ref>{{Cite web|title=The ultimate luxury spa guide to
