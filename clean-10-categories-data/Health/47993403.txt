'''''Health, Risk & Society''''' is a [[peer-reviewed]] [[academic journal]] covering all aspects of [[Quantitative risk analysis|risk analysis]] concerning health issues. It was established in 1999 and is published by [[Taylor & Francis]]. The [[editor-in-chief]] is Patrick Brown from the University of Amsterdam, Netherlands.
