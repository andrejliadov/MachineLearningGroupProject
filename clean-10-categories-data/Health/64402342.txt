The [[European Platform for Patients’ Organisations, Science & Industry]], based in [[Brussels]] was established in 1994 as part of the initiative to promote patients' rights.<ref>{{cite journal |title=Patients Rights in Europe |journal=European Journal of Health Law |date=2000 |volume=7 |pages=1 |url=https://heinonline.org/HOL/LandingPage?handle=hein.journals/eurjhlb7&div=8&id=&page= |accessdate=28 June 2020}}</ref>  It is an independent, not-for-profit, partnership-based and multi-stakeholder think tank.<ref>{{cite web |title=European Platform for Patients’ Organisations, Science and Industry |url=https://wfip.org/epposi/ |website=World Federation of Incontinence Patients |accessdate=28 June 2020}}</ref>   It is now also known as Digital Health Europe and is part of the [[ European Patients Forum]]. <ref>{{cite news |title=Digital Health Europe |url=https://www.eu-patient.eu/whatwedo/Projects/ongoing-projects/digitalhealtheurope/#:~:text=Digital%20Health%20Europe%20(DHE)%20is,of%20the%20Digital%20Single%20Market. |accessdate=28 June 2020 |publisher=EU Patient |date=2020}}</ref>

It is particularly concerned with [[Biobank]]ing, geno-type research.  It involves a number of European-facing umbrella patients’ organizations including patients' organisations, commercial enterprises and their related trade bodies, research institutes, professional and business federations.<ref>{{cite news |title=European Platform for Patients’ Organisations, Science and Industry |url=https://lobbyfacts.eu/representative/01d1dd6f66f04917b93d68163715a35e/european-platform-for-patients-organisations-science-and-industry |accessdate=28 June 2020 |publisher=Lobby Facts |date=2016}}</ref>

John Peter Mary Wubbe, a Swiss transplant patient, is the Secretary General. <ref>{{cite news |title=John Peter Mary Wubbe |url=http://bettersciencebetterhealth.com/john-wubbe/ |accessdate=28 June 2020 |publisher=Better Science, Better Health |date=2018}}</ref>
