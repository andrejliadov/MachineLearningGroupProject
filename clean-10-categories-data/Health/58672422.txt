
'''Agenesis of the vena cava''' is a genetic [[agenesis|defect]] of the inferior [[vena cava]]. It comes about when, during development of the [[foetus]], the right [[subcardinal vein]] does not connect as it should to the [[hepatic sinusoids]].{{r|ben|elliot|page2=628}}.
