'''Victor M. A. Aguirre-Torres''' is an internationally recognized [[econometrics|econometrician]], professor and researcher<ref>{{cite web|title=List of credited researchers by Mexico's National System of Researchers|url=http://www.conacyt.gob.mx/images//SNI/VIGENTES_SNI2014.xlsx|work= Mexico's National System of Researchers official website|accessdate=14 August 2014}}</ref> of the Academic Department of [[Statistics]] at the [[Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México]] (ITAM).<ref>{{cite web|title=List of ITAM's Faculty members|url=http://estadistica.itam.mx/es/facultad-dep-estadistica|publisher=Intituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México (ITAM)|accessdate=15 August 2014}}</ref>  He is a member of the [[Mexican Academy of Sciences]] (Academia Mexicana de Ciencias). <ref>{{cite web|title=List of members admitted to the Mexican Academy of Sciences|url=http://www.amc.edu.mx/amc/NUEVOSMIEMBROS2014.pdf|publisher=Academia Mexicana de Ciencias (AMC)|accessdate=30 November 2014}}</ref>  Since 1991, he has formed a significant number of leaders in México, with nearly 46 generations to his credit.

Besides his family, he has a passion for teaching and research in statistics and econometrics. He has over 33 publications in [[Academic journal|research journals]] and 23 distinctions, among which stand out, those obtained as the Best Paper Award of The 2009 International Conference of Computational Statistics and Data Engineering, in London, England;<ref>{{cite web|title=World Congress on Engineering 2009|url=http://www.iaeng.org/WCE2009/congress_awards.html|publisher=WCE 2009 organized by the International Association of Engineers (IAENG)|accessdate=14 March 2014}}</ref> and being Listed in Who’s Who in the World 2010, USA.<ref>{{cite web|title=MARQUIS Who'sWho|url=http://marquiswhoswho.com/|publisher=MARQUIS Who'sWho|accessdate=14 March 2014}}</ref>  He has been a member of the [[Sistema Nacional de Investigadores|National Accreditation Board of Research]] (SNI), area of Physics and Mathematics, since 1988.<ref>{{cite web|title=National Accreditation Board of Research|url=http://www.conacyt.mx/index.php/el-conacyt/sistema-nacional-de-investigadores|publisher=Mexico's National Accreditation Board of Research|accessdate=14 March 2014}}</ref>

In 1982, he received the Ph.D degree in Statistics from [[North Carolina State University]] (NCSU).<ref>{{cite web|title=Mathematics Genealogy Project|url=http://genealogy.math.ndsu.nodak.edu/id.php?id=52581|publisher=Department of Mathematics, North Dakota State University PO Box 6050 Fargo, North Dakota 581086050|accessdate=14 August 2014}}</ref> Before that he obtained a Master of Statistics from NCSU and a Master of Science in Mathematics from the National Polytechnic Institute, Mexico City. His Alma Mater is the [[Instituto Politécnico Nacional|National Polytechnic Institute]], Mexico City, where he earned a Physics and Mathematics degree.<ref>{{cite web|title=Diploma (Cédula) number 507513|url=http://cedula.buholegal.com/507513/|publisher= Buholegal's official site|accessdate=14 August 2014}}</ref>

He has given more than 33 international seminars and has been invited to over 32 talks and 47 contributed talks.

Among his achievements, he was leader in the creation of the Statistics Group at the [[Centro de Investigación en Matemáticas|Center for Research in Mathematics]] in Guanajuato (CIMAT).
