{{short description|Annual award for achievements in Latvian–Lithuanian culture}}
The '''Balts' Award''' ({{lang-lv|Baltu balva}}, {{lang-lt|Baltų apdovanojimas}}) is an annual award given to recognise excellence and achievements in the areas of [[Latvia]]n-[[Lithuania]]n culture, history and language.<ref name="MFA-Lith1">{{cite web |title=The contest announced for the first Balts’ Award |url=https://www.urm.lt/default/en/news/the-contest-announced-for-the-first-balts-award |publisher=[[Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Lithuania]] |accessdate=8 October 2018 |date=12 April 2018}}</ref><ref name="TBT-1">{{cite news |title=Latvia, Lithuania establish Balts’ Award |url=https://www.baltictimes.com/latvia__lithuania_establish_balts__award/ |accessdate=8 October 2018 |newspaper=[[The Baltic Times]] |date=12 January 2018}}</ref><ref name="BC-28Sept2018">{{cite news |title=Latvian and Lithuanian foreign ministers to present the first Balts Award next week |url=http://www.baltic-course.com/eng2/good_for_business/?doc=143763 |accessdate=8 October 2018 |newspaper=[[The Baltic Course]] |date=28 September 2018}}</ref>

The award was inaugurated in 2018.<ref name="LSM-4Oct2018">{{cite news |title=First 'Balts award' winner named |url=https://eng.lsm.lv/article/culture/culture/first-balts-award-winner-named.a294678/ |accessdate=8 October 2018 |publisher=[[Public Broadcasting of Latvia]] |date=4 October 2018}}</ref><ref name="DELFI-4Oct2018">{{cite news |title=Linguist Balodė receives 1st Balts' award in ceremony in Riga |url=https://en.delfi.lt/lithuania/foreign-affairs/linguist-balode-receives-1st-balts-award-in-ceremony-in-riga.d?id=79227751 |accessdate=8 October 2018 |publisher=[[Delfi (web portal)|Delfi]] |agency=BNS |date=4 October 2018}}</ref>
