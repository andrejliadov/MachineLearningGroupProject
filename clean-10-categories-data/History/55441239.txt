'''''A History of the Modern World''''' is a work initially published by the distinguished American historian at Princeton and Yale universities [[Robert Roswell Palmer]] in 1950. The work has since been extended by [[Joel Colton]] (from its second edition, 1956)<ref>The preface for the first Swedish edition 1960, by Torvald Höjer</ref> and Lloyd S. Kramer (from its ninth edition, 2001),<ref name="amazon.com">https://www.amazon.com/History-Modern-World-9th/dp/0375413987</ref> and currently (as of October 2017) counts thirteen editions.<ref>the 11th (under the title "A History of Europe in the Modern World") is shown at [https://www.amazon.co.uk/History-Europe-Modern-World/dp/0073385549], the ISBN numbers must refer to the 10th and 13th editions</ref><ref name="amazon.com"/> First published in 1950, translated into six languages, is used in more than 1,000 colleges and universities as well as many high school advanced placement courses.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2002/06/18/us/r-r-palmer-93-history-text-author-dies.html|title=R. R. Palmer, 93, History Text Author, Dies|first=Douglas|last=Martin|date=18 June 2002|publisher=|via=NYTimes.com}}</ref>

The second edition (1956), comprises two volumes, 20 main chapters and 110 sub-chapters.<ref>Swedish Translation, R.R. Palmer, Scandinavian University Books, "Nya Tidens Världshistoria", edited by Torvald Höjer, Stockholm 1960, no ISBN (also published in Danish, by Gyldendalske Boghandel / Nordisk Forlag, Copenhagen)</ref> The author focuses on World History from a European perspective, and the newer editions also exists under the title "A History of Europe in the Modern World".<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.amazon.co.uk/History-Europe-Modern-World/dp/0073385549|title=A History of Europe in the Modern World|first1=R. R.|last1=Palmer|first2=Joel|last2=Colton|first3=Lloyd|last3=Kramer|date=16 October 2013|publisher=McGraw-Hill Education|via=Amazon}}</ref>
