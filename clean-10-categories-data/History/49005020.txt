The '''World Monuments Watch''' is a flagship advocacy program of the [[New York City|New York]]-based private [[non-profit organization]] [[World Monuments Fund]] (WMF) that calls international attention to [[cultural heritage]] around the world that is threatened by [[neglect]], [[vandalism]], [[war|conflict]], or [[natural disaster|disaster]].<ref name=press_release>{{cite web | title = World Monuments Fund Announces 2014 World Monuments Watch,Identifying Cultural Heritage Sites at Risk | author = World Monuments Fund | date = 8 October 2013 | publisher = World Monuments Fund | url =
https://www.wmf.org/press-release/world-monuments-fund-announces-2014-world-monuments-watchidentifying-cultural-heritage|accessdate=4 January 2016}}</ref>
