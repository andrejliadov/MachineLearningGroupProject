'''''Journal of the Philosophy of History''''' (JPH) is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] academic philosophy journal focusing on the [[philosophy of history]] and [[Historiography|historical theory]] published three times a year, by [[Brill Publishers]]. Journal of the Philosophy of History is edited and institutionally anchored at the Centre for Philosophical Studies of History of the [[University of Oulu]], Finland. It is one of a few journals with an explicit focus on philosophical issues pertaining to history and historiography. The journal contains original research articles, book reviews, and extended review essays. 

JPH was founded in 2007 by the Dutch philosopher of history [[Frank Ankersmit]].<ref> {{Cite web|title=The Philosophy of History: An Agenda|url=https://brill.com/view/journals/jph/1/1/jph.1.issue-1.xml|website=brill.com|language=en|access-date=2020-05-23}}</ref> Since 2017 it is under the editorship of Jouni-Matti Kuukkanen,<ref>{{Cite web|title=Welcome Note from Editor-in-Chief |url=https://brill.com/view/journals/jph/11/2/article-p122_2.xml|website=www.brill.com|language=en|access-date=2020-05-24}}</ref> a philosopher of history and science and director of the Centre for Philosophical Studies of History at the University of Oulu.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Jouni-Matti Kuukkanen|url=https://www.oulu.fi/university/researcher/jouni-matti-kuukkanen|website=www.oulu.fi|language=en|access-date=2020-05-23}}</ref> The editorial board consists of Giuseppina D'Oro<ref> {{Cite web|title=Giuseppina D'Oro |url=https://www.keele.ac.uk/spgs/staff/giuseppinadoro/|website=keele.ac.uk |language=en|access-date=2020-05-24}}</ref> (Keele University), Allan Megill<ref> {{Cite web|title=Allan Megill |url=https://history.virginia.edu/people/profile/adm9e|website=virginia.edu|access-date=2020-05-24}}</ref> (University of Virginia), Marek Tamm<ref> {{Cite web|title=Marek Tamm |url=https://www.tlu.ee/en/node/105928 |website=tlu.ee |language=en|access-date=2020-05-24}}</ref> (Tallinn University), and Herman Paul<ref> {{Cite web|title=Herman Paul |url=https://www.universiteitleiden.nl/en/staffmembers/herman-paul#tab-1 |website=universiteitleiden.nl |language=en|access-date=2020-05-24}}</ref> (Leiden University). Eugen Zeleňák<ref> {{Cite web|title=Eugen Zeleňák |url=https://sites.google.com/site/eugenzelenak/ |website=sites.google.com |language=en|access-date=2020-05-24}}</ref> (Catholic University in Ruzomberok) is review editor, and Georg Gangl<ref> {{Cite web|title=Georg Gangl |url=https://www.oulu.fi/university/researcher/georg-gangl |website=oulu.fi |language=en|access-date=2020-05-24}}</ref> (University of Oulu) acts as editorial assistant.

The journal is indexed in [[Scopus]]<ref> {{Cite web|title=Source details Journal of the History of Philosophy|url=https://www.scopus.com/sourceid/16800154742|website=scopus.com|language=en|access-date=2020-05-23}}</ref> and [[Web of Science]].<ref> {{Cite web|title= Web of Science|url=https://publons.com/publon/?journal=15454&order_by=date|website=publons.com|language=en|access-date=2020-05-23}}</ref>
