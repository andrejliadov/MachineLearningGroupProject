'''''Gods in the Sky''''' was a three-part British television series on [[Channel 4]], covering astronomical religion in early civilizations.<ref>[http://www.channel4.com/history/microsites/G/gods_in_sky/ ''Gods in the Sky''], [[Channel 4]].</ref> It is presented by the historian of science [[Allan Chapman (historian)|Allan Chapman]]. The series was filmed in Britain, [[Egypt]], [[Greece]], and Italy.<ref>[http://www.wagtv.com/programme/Gods-In-The-Sky-311.html ''Gods in the Sky''] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080705133013/http://www.wagtv.com/programme/Gods-in-the-Sky-311.html |date=5 July 2008 }}, WAG TV.</ref>

There is a book of the same name to accompany the series.<ref>[[Allan Chapman (historian)|Allan Chapman]], ''Gods in the Sky: Astronomy from the Ancients to the Enlightenment''. [[Channel 4 Books]], 2002. {{ISBN|978-0-7522-6164-5}}.</ref>
