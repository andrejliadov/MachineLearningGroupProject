The '''Richard Dawkins Award''' is an annual prize recognizing those who exemplify values of [[secularism]] and [[rationalism]].<ref name="cfi 2019">{{cite web |title=RICHARD DAWKINS AWARD MOVING TO THE CENTER FOR INQUIRY |url=https://centerforinquiry.org/press_releases/richard-dawkins-award-moving-to-the-center-for-inquiry/ |publisher=Center for Inquiry |accessdate=1 August 2019}}</ref> Named in honor of evolutionary biologist [[Richard Dawkins]], the prize was first established by the [[Atheist Alliance of America]] in 2003, and since 2019 has been presented by the [[Center for Inquiry]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://atheistallianceamerica.org/the-richard-dawkins-award/|title=The Richard Dawkins Award|website=SERVING THE ATHEIST COMMUNITY FOR MORE THAN 20 YEARS|language=en-US|access-date=2016-06-25}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.moneycontrol.com/news/photos/india/in-pics-richard-dawkins-award-past-winners-javed-akhtar-to-stephen-fry-5373551.html|title=In pics  Richard Dawkins Award past winners - Javed Akhtar to Stephen Fry}}</ref><ref name=theguardian.com>{{cite web|title=Richard Dawkins named world's top thinker in poll|url=https://www.theguardian.com/books/booksblog/2013/apr/25/richard-dawkins-named-top-thinker|publisher=theguardian.com|accessdate=6 October 2013}}</ref>
