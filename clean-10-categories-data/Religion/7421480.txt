The '''Swedish Evangelical Mission''' ('''SEM''') ([[Swedish language|Swedish]]: ''Evangeliska Fosterlands-Stiftelsen'' "Evangelical Homeland Foundation", EFS) is an independent movement within the [[Church of Sweden]]. Established on 7 May [[1856]],<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.efs.nu/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/I-detta-tecken-EFS-150-%C3%A5r.pdf|title=I detta tecken|publisher=Swedish Evangelical Mission|language=Swedish|date=2006|access-date=3 October 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151004122603/http://www.efs.nu/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/I-detta-tecken-EFS-150-%C3%A5r.pdf|archive-date=4 October 2015|df=}}</ref> it cooperates with independent churches, Christian councils and other partners in [[Eritrea]], [[Ethiopia]], [[India]], [[Malawi]], the [[Sudan]] and [[Tanzania]]. The focus is on education, relationships and to communicate the [[gospel]].
