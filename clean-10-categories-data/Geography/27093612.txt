[[Colombia]] has one [[time zone]], '''Colombia Time (COT)''', which is located in the [[UTCâˆ’05:00]] zone, 5 hours behind [[Coordinated Universal Time]] (UTC). Its standard time zone abbreviation is <code>COT</code>.<ref>Worldtimezone.com [http://www.worldtimezone.com/wtz-names/wtz-cot.html Time zone names - Colombia Time], Retrieved April 24, 2010</ref>

Colombia does not observe [[daylight saving time]], but [[Daylight saving time in Colombia|used it during eleven months]] between May 1992 and April 1993.
