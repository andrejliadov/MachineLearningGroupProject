'''''Australian Geographer''''' ('''''The Australian Geographer''''' until 1975) is a quarterly [[peer-reviewed]] [[academic journal]] published by the Geographical Society of New South Wales since August 1928.{{sfn|Biddle|Aplin|1996}} Covering all aspects of [[Australian geography]], it is currently copublished with [[Taylor & Francis]].
