'''Goljak''' ({{lang-sr-Cyrl|Гољак}}, {{lang-sq|Gollak}}) is a mountainous region in the eastern part of [[Kosovo]]{{efn|name=status}}, bordering the Lab region to the west. The cities of [[Pristina]] and [[Gjilan]] in Kosovo, and [[Sijarinska Banja]] in Serbia are located by the mountains. The highest peak ''Velja glava'' has an elevation of {{convert|1181|m|ft|0|abbr=on}} above sea level.
