The '''84th parallel north''' is a [[circle of latitude]] that is 84 [[degree (angle)|degrees]] [[true north|north]] of the [[Earth|Earth's]] [[equator|equatorial plane]], in the [[Arctic]]. It passes solely through the [[Arctic Ocean]].

The [[Extreme points of Earth#Latitude and longitude|northernmost]] land on earth, [[Kaffeklubben Island]], [[Greenland]] is roughly 40 kilometres south of this parallel.  

At this latitude the [[sun]] is visible for 24 hours, 0 minutes during the [[summer solstice]] and [[Twilight#Astronomical twilight|astronomical twilight]] during the [[winter solstice]].
