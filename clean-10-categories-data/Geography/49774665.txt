'''''Environment and Urbanization ASIA''''' is a [[peer review]]ed journal which provides information in the fields of urbanization, human settlements and the environment across Asia. It is published twice a year by [http://www.sagepub.in/home.nav SAGE Publications] in association with [[National Institute of Urban Affairs]]. Its audience includes researchers, academicians, policy-makers, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), activists and students particularly in Asia.
