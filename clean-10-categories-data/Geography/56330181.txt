The Central American Pacific Islands is a [[biogeography|biogeographical]] area used in the [[World Geographical Scheme for Recording Plant Distributions]]. It has the Level 3 code "CPI". It consists of a number of islands off the western coast of [[Central America]] in the [[Pacific Ocean]]: [[Clipperton Island]], [[Cocos Island]] and [[Malpelo Island]].<ref name=WGSRPD/>

Clipperton Island is the most north-westerly, lying off [[Nicaragua]] and [[Costa Rica]]. Politically it belongs to [[France]].<ref name=WWF/> Cocos Island and Malpelo Island lie south of [[Panama]], although Cocos Island belongs to Costa Rica<ref name=HoguMill81/> and Malpelo Island to Colombia.<ref name=Schn/>
