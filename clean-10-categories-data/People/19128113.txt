'''Prince Panduvasudeva''', (? -474 BC)was King of [[Kingdom of Upatissa Nuwara|Upatissa Nuwara]] (modern-day [[Sri Lanka]]) from 504 BC to 474 BC. He was the first monarch of the [[Kingdom of Upatissa Nuwara]] succeeding [[Upatissa of Upatissa Nuwara|Upatissa]], who reigned as regent. Panduvasudeva had ten sons, including [[Abhaya of Upatissa Nuwara|Abhaya]], [[Tissa of Upatissa Nuwara|Tissa]] and his only daughter Ummadha Citta. He was a nephew of [[Prince Vijaya]].{{Citation needed|date=June 2016}}
