'''Artavasdes IV''' (also spelled '''Artabasdes''') was a legendary figure, who is mentioned as the "king of the Armenians" under the [[Sasanian Empire|Sasanian]] monarch [[Shapur I]] ({{reign|240|270}}) in the ''[[Historia Augusta]]''.{{sfn|Chaumont|1986|pp=418-438}}
