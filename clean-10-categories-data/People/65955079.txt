'''Cinema and Death: on Image and Immortality''' is a Persian book on [[philosophy of film]], written by Milad Roshani Payan.<ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Cinema and Death in Google Books|url=https://books.google.com/books/about/%D8%B3%D9%8A%D9%86%D9%85%D8%A7_%D9%88_%D9%85%D8%B1%DA%AF.html?id=kTj9xAEACAAJ|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-11-27|website=Google Books}}</ref> in February 2020, the book won the highest award in the most prestigious book award in Iran, called [[Iran's Book of the Year Awards]], in dramatic arts category.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-02-05|title=Iran honors top books of the year|url=https://www.tehrantimes.com/news/444899/Iran-honors-top-books-of-the-year|access-date=2020-11-27|website=Tehran Times|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Iranian top books of the year|url=https://czech.mfa.gov.ir/en/newsview/572758|url-status=live|archive-url=https://czech.mfa.gov.ir/en/newsview/572758|archive-date=2020-02-07|access-date=2020-11-27|website=Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Iran|language=en}}</ref>

== summary ==
The book is divided into six chapters, and in each chapter, the relationship between cinema and death is examined.
