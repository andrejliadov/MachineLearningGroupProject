'''Nader Afshar Alavinejad''' ({{lang-fa|نادر افشارعلوی‌نژاد}}, is a former Iranian football player. He played for the [[Iran national football team]] in the [[Football at the 1951 Asian Games|1951 Asian Games]] and the [[Football at the 1958 Asian Games|1958 Asian Games]].<ref>http://www.teammelli.com/matchdata/details/player.php?id=94</ref>

He previously played for the [[Docharkheh Savaran]] and [[Taj SC|Taj]] until 1952.<ref>http://www.national-football-teams.com/player/30403/Nader_Afshar_Alavinejad.html</ref>
