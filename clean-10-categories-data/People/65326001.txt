The '''Asia Game Changer Awards''' is an annual award ceremony held in recognition of individuals and organizations within and connected to the Asian community that have made positive contributions to the development and improvement of Asia and society.

Inaugurated in 2014 by New York-based non-profit organization the [[Asia Society]], in partnership with [[Citibank]], the first ceremony was held at the [[Headquarters of the United Nations|United Nations headquarters]] in [[New York City]].<ref name="NBC News">{{cite web|url=https://www.nbcnews.com/news/ncna205706|title=Jack Ma, Malala Among Asia Society's 'Game Changer' Honorees|author=<!--Not stated-->|date=September 18, 2014|work=[[NBC News]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200915191537/https://www.nbcnews.com/news/amp/ncna205706|archive-date=September 15, 2020|url-status=dead|access-date=September 15, 2020}}</ref> A total of thirteen honorees were awarded, including [[List of Nobel laureates|Nobel laureate]] [[Malala Yousafzai]] and internet entrepreneur [[Jack Ma]], who was honoured as the first ever Game Changer of the Year.<ref name="NBC News"/> Nominees are selected from individuals and institutions nominated from the Asia Society's global network,<ref name="NBC News"/> which then embarks on a three-month long process of feedback and voting that results in the final awardees.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://asiasociety.org/asia-game-changers/about|title=About|website=asiasociety.org|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140913231615/https://asiasociety.org/asia-game-changers/about|archive-date=September 13, 2014|url-status=live|access-date=October 9, 2020}}</ref> While recipients are usually from varying diverse backgrounds, the 2019 ceremony marked the first time that all awardees for the year were women only.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gulftoday.ae/news/2019/09/12/hoor-al-qasimi-2019-asia-societys-game-changer|title=Hoor Al Qasimi: 2019 Asia Society's Game Changer|last=Jara-Puyod|first=Mariecar|date=September 12, 2019|work=[[Gulf Today]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200917195717/https://www.gulftoday.ae/news/2019/09/12/hoor-al-qasimi-2019-asia-societys-game-changer|archive-date=September 17, 2019|url-status=live|access-date=September 17, 2020}}</ref> 

Due to the on-going [[COVID-19 pandemic]], the 2020 ceremony was held online.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/magazines/panache/chef-vikas-khanna-honoured-with-asia-game-changer-award-for-feeding-millions-in-india-amid-covid-crisis/articleshow/78160518.cms|title=Chef Vikas Khanna honoured with Asia Game Changer award for feeding millions in India amid Covid crisis|author=<!--Not stated-->|date=September 17, 2019|work=[[The Economic Times]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200923045730/https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/magazines/panache/chef-vikas-khanna-honoured-with-asia-game-changer-award-for-feeding-millions-in-india-amid-covid-crisis/articleshow/78160518.cms?from=mdr|archive-date=September 23, 2019|url-status=live|access-date=September 23, 2020}}</ref>
