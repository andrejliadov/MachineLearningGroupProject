A '''commercial director''' is a [[film director]] who specializes in the creation of [[commercials]], often for television, but sometimes also on film and of  [[music video]]s (given that music videos are recognized as a form of advertising for the song featured in the video{{citation needed|date=November 2015}}).

The term is also used for the more general business role of [[chief commercial officer]].

==Noted commercial directors==
Noted directors of commercials include: [[Ridley Scott]], [[Adrian Lyne]], [[Hugh Hudson]], [[Jean-Paul Goude]], [[Jonathan Glazer]], [[Michel Gondry]], [[Spike Jonze]], [[Joe Pytka]], [[Tarsem Singh]], [[Dougal Wilson]] and [[Tony Kaye (director)|Tony Kaye]], Norman Hafezi, [[Barry Myers (director)|Barry Myers]].{{citation needed|date=November 2015}} Music video directors who have gone on to feature film production include [[Michael Mann (director)|Michael Mann]] and [[Julien Temple]].<ref>https://www.theguardian.com/film/2015/jul/19/ecstasy-wilko-johnson-review-mark-kermode</ref>
