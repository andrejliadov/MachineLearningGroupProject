'''De Dee Hame Azaadi''' (Miraculously given us freedom) or '''Sabarmati ke Sant'''  is an Indian song written by [[Kavi Pradeep]]. It is a patriotic song dedicated to [[Mahatma Gandhi]] and his non-violence nature.<ref>{{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=q7OEAwAAQBAJ&pg=PR9 |title=Mahatma a Scientist of the Intuitively Obvious |author= Pramod Kumar Sharma |publisher= Partridge Publishing|page = 9 |isbn=9781482819236}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Driving home a point through songs |url= http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/tp-karnataka/driving-home-a-point-through-songs/article1350881.ece |newspaper= [[The Hindu]] | date= 4 October 2008 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Tributes through songs |url=http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/tributes-through-songs/article1922808.ece |author=Shyamhari Chakra |date=3 October 2007 |newspaper= The Hindu}}</ref> This a film soundtrack of Bollywood film ''[[Jagriti]]'' (1954). This song was sung by [[Asha Bhosle]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Jagriti 1954 |url=http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-features/tp-metroplus/jagriti-1954/article640322.ece |newspaper=The Hindu |date=13 June 2009 |author = Vijay Lokapally }}</ref>
