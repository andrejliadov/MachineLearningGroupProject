'''''The Great Warrior Skanderbeg''''' ({{lang-al|Luftëtari i madh i Shqipërisë Skënderbeu}}; {{lang-ru|Великий воин Албании Скандербег|Velikiy voin Albanii Skanderbeg}}) is a 1953 Soviet-Albanian [[biopic]] directed by [[Sergei Yutkevich]]. It was entered into the [[1954 Cannes Film Festival]] where it earned the [[1954 Cannes Film Festival#International Prize|International Prize]]. Yutkevich also earned the [[1954 Cannes Film Festival#Special Mention|Special Mention]] award for his direction.<ref name="festival-cannes.com">{{cite web |url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/archives/ficheFilm/id/3875/year/1954.html |title=Festival de Cannes: The Great Warrior Skanderbeg |accessdate=31 January 2009|work=festival-cannes.com}}</ref>

The film is a biography of [[Skanderbeg|George Kastriot Skanderbeg]] (1405 – 1468), widely known as Skanderbeg, a 15th-century [[Albanians|Albanian]] lord who defended his land against the [[Ottoman Empire]] for more than two decades.

In 2012, for the [[100th Anniversary of the Independence of Albania|100th anniversary of Albanian independence]], the film was remastered for high definition with new voices, music, and sound effects.<ref name="100thAn">{{cite web |url=http://www.top-channel.tv/artikull.php?id=246781 |title=Skenderbeu: HD |accessdate=20 May 2014|work=Top Channel}}</ref>
