'''Alisa Ahmann''' is a German fashion model.

==Career==
Ahmann and her sisters (including twin Xenia) were discovered in [[Stuttgart]], [[Germany]]. She debuted as a [[Calvin Klein Collection]] exclusive in 2014 and opened their F/W show. Within a year she did campaigns for [[Gucci]] and [[Alberta Ferretti]], and walked for [[Hermès]], [[Chloé]], and [[Oscar de la Renta]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.vogue.com/article/alisa-ahmann-model-interview-calvin-klein-exclusive|title=Watch This Face: The Rise of Model Alisa Ahmann|work=Vogue}}</ref> She has also walked for [[Dior]], [[Jil Sander]], [[Donna Karan]], [[Elie Saab]], [[Stella McCartney]], [[Valentino (fashion house)|Valentino]], [[Miu Miu]], [[Alexander McQueen]], and [[Giambattista Valli]].

Ahmann has been on the cover of [[Elle (magazine)|Elle Germany]], [[List of Vogue Deutsch cover models|Vogue Germany]], and [[Vogue Italia]] among others.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.elle.de/alisa-ahmann-interview|title=September-Covermodel Alisa Ahmann im Interview: "Modeln ist wie eine Therapie"|work=Elle Deutschland}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://models.com/work/vogue-italia-waiting-for-the-cold-wave|title=Vogue Italia November 2016  Beauty Cover|work=Models.com}}</ref> She has appeared in magazines such as ''[[W (magazine)|W]]''<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.wmagazine.com/story/german-model-alisa-ahmann-beauty|title=German Model Alisa Ahmann Has Plenty of Advice When It Comes to Indie Beauty Brands|work=W}}</ref> Vogue Brasil, ''Self Service''<ref>{{cite web|url=http://selfservicemagazine.com/credit/alisa-ahmann/|title=Alisa Ahmann|work=Self Service}}</ref> [[Vogue Paris]], and [[Allure (magazine)|Allure]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.fashionmodeldirectory.com/models/alisa_ahmann/|title=Alisa Ahmann|work=FMD}}</ref>

Ahmann was once ranked as a "Top 50" model by models.com<ref>{{cite web|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170129144040/https://models.com/rankings/ui/Top50/|archive-date=January 29, 2017|url=http://models.com/rankings/ui/Top50|title=Top 50 Models|website=Models.com|access-date=February 19, 2019|quote=A breathtaking new face destined for the big leagues, Alisa Ahmann debuted as a Calvin Klein exclusive and headed straight for campaign work by Craig McDean for the bastion of refinement that is Tiffany & Co. In short order Alisa was snapped up by prestige brands like Gucci and Alberta Ferreti while still finding time to do editorial duty in Vogue Paris, Vogue Japan and i-D.}}</ref>
