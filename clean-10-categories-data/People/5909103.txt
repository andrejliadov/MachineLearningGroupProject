In [[Australian]] [[Aboriginal Mythology]], '''Ankotarinja''' is the first man, also called the Dreaming Man. His myth belonged to a small band of [[Arrernte people|Arrernte]] men, known as the [[Ngala-Mbitjana]] people. They were heirs to Ankotarinja's exploits at the time of the [[Dreaming (spirituality)|Dreaming]], performing his ceremonies and initiating their young [[iliara]] (novice) into his totemic lodge. They kept alive the memory of his world-creating endeavors. Ankotarinja's birthplace is a creek-bed near [[Ankota]] (vicinity of [[Mount Solitaire]]).{{cn|date=June 2012}}
