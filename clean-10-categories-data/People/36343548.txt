'''Sandra Aleksejeva''' (born 3 May 1991) is a [[Latvia]]n BMX racer. She has qualified for [[2012 Summer Olympics]] in [[London]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://sportacentrs.com/ritenbrauksana/bmx/04062012-veide_ieklauts_latvijas_sastava_olimpiade|title=Veide iekļauts Latvijas sastāvā olimpiādes BMX sacensībām|publisher=Sportacentrs.com|language=Latvian|accessdate=6 July 2012}}</ref> Aleksejeva has participated in several European and World Championships. Aleksejeva was born in [[Krāslava]], [[Latvia]].
