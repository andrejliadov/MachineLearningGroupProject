'''Legal biography''' is the [[biography]] of persons relevant to [[law]]. In a preface dated October 1983, [[A. W. B. Simpson]] wrote that it was "a rather neglected field".<ref>A W B Simpson (editor). [[Biographical Dictionary of the Common Law]]. Butterworths. London. 1984. {{ISBN|0 406 51657 X}}. Page v</ref> Since then there has been a "resurgence of interest".<ref>Parry, R Gwynedd. Is Legal Biography Really Legal Scholarship? (June 2010) Legal Studies: The Journal of the [[Society of Legal Scholars]]. Volume 30. Issue 2. Page 208. Wiley Online Library. {{doi|10.1111/j.1748-121X.2009.00149.x}}</ref>
