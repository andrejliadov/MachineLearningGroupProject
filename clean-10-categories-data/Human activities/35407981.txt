'''''Azure''''' is a media brand covering architecture and design published in [[Toronto]], [[Ontario]], Canada.<ref>{{cite news|author=Laura Serra|title=AZURE magazine 25th anniversary|url=https://www.theglobeandmail.com/news/toronto/azure-magazine-25th-anniversary/article1380684/|accessdate=23 December 2015|work=The Globe and Mail|date=8 October 2010}}</ref>  ''Azure'' is described as "an indispensable resource for architects, designers and the design-savvy public" on its website.<ref>{{cite web|title=About Azure|url=http://www.azuremagazine.com/about/index.php|work=Azure|accessdate=9 April 2012}}</ref> It was founded in 1985 by Nelda Rodger and Sergio Sgaramella.<ref>{{cite web|title=AZURE magazine questionnaire: editor-in-chief nelda rodger |url=http://www.designboom.com/weblog/cat/9/view/12707/azure-magazine-questionnaire-editor-in-chief-nelda-rodger.html |work=designboom |date=2011 |accessdate=9 April 2012 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20120423045455/http://www.designboom.com/weblog/cat/9/view/12707/azure-magazine-questionnaire-editor-in-chief-nelda-rodger.html |archivedate=23 April 2012 }}</ref> In 2000, it won the [[Canadian National Magazine Awards]]' ''Magazine of the Year''.<ref>{{cite web|title=Magazine of the Year: 2000|url=http://www.magazine-awards.com/index.cfm?ci_id=1397&la_id=1|work=National Magazine Awards|accessdate=9 April 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120207110827/http://www.magazine-awards.com/index.cfm?ci_id=1397&la_id=1|archive-date=2012-02-07|url-status=dead}}</ref> In 2011, the magazine launched the '''AZ Awards''', an international competition open to architects, landscape architects, designers, students, clients and manufacturers.<ref>[http://www.azuremagazine.com/article/1st-annual-az-award-winners-announced/ 1st annual AZ awards winner announced] June 16, 2011 Azure</ref> In February 2013, deputy editor Catherine Osborne was named the magazine's editor in chief, with founding editor Nelda Rodger remaining as editorial director for Azure Publishing.<ref>[http://www.azuremagazine.com/article/azure-magazine-announces-new-editor Azure Magazine]</ref>
