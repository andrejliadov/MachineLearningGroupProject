'''AirportWatch''' is an environmental group which campaigns for sustainable air transport through reduction or redistribution of demand, determined by either timing or location ([[Transportation Demand Management|Demand Management]] approach).

==History==
AirportWatch was formed in 2000.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.airportwatch.org.uk|title=AirportWatch website|accessdate=2007-12-27|publisher=AirportWatch| archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20080113150200/http://www.airportwatch.org.uk/| archivedate= 13 January 2008 | url-status= live}}</ref>

In the run-up to the 2007 [[Camp for Climate Action]] near [[Heathrow Airport]], [[BAA Limited|BAA]] sought an injunction, banning members of groups supporting AirportWatch, such as the [[National Trust for Places of Historic Interest or Natural Beauty|National Trust]] and [[Greenpeace]], from attending the protest.<ref>{{cite news|title=Catch-all Heathrow protest injunction could bar millions|url=https://www.theguardian.com/environment/2007/jul/27/climatechange?gusrc=rss&feed=11|accessdate=2007-12-27|work=[[The Guardian]]|date=2007-07-27 | location=London | first=John | last=Vidal}}</ref> The airport operator won a reduced injunction naming senior members of AirportWatch and [[Plane Stupid]], but not including AirportWatch or its members.<ref>{{cite web|title=Heathrow airport's operator wins injunction to stop protesters|url=http://www.iht.com/articles/ap/2007/08/06/europe/EU-GEN-Britain-Heathrow-Protests.php| accessdate=2007-12-27|date=2007-08-06|work=[[International Herald Tribune]]}}</ref>

AirportWatch is a member of the [[Stop Climate Chaos]] Coalition.<ref>{{cite news|title=Boom in Scots air travel set to bust emissions targets |url=http://www.sundayherald.com/news/heraldnews/display.var.2295792.0.boom_in_scots_air_travel_set_to_bust_emissions_targets.php |work=[[Sunday Herald]] |date=8 April 2009 |accessdate=12 May 2009 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20090209151529/http://www.sundayherald.com/news/heraldnews/display.var.2295792.0.boom_in_scots_air_travel_set_to_bust_emissions_targets.php |archivedate=9 February 2009 }}</ref>

==Campaign Issues==
These campaign issues have been identified by AirportWatch:<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.airportwatch.org.uk/problems.php|title=Issues in a nutshell|accessdate=2007-12-27|publisher=AirportWatch|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20080110034043/http://www.airportwatch.org.uk/problems.php|archivedate=10 January 2008|url-status=dead|df=dmy-all}}</ref>

* [[Noise]] complaints by local communities near [[airports]]
* Environmental impact near airports
* Growth in low-cost holiday flights affecting the UK economy
* Possible environmental effects from aviation
* Effectiveness of [[carbon offsetting]] in removing CO2 from the atmosphere
