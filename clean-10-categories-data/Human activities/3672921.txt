An '''alanorarius''', in ancient [[Europe]]an customs, was a keeper or manager of [[spaniel]]s, or setting-dogs, for the sport of [[hunting]], [[falconry]], etc.

The word is formed from the [[Gothic language|Gothic]] ''Alan'', a [[greyhound]].
