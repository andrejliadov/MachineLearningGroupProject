'''Cat lover culture''' describes the culture that surrounds [[cat]] lovers. For some it can become an obsession and some people advertise the fact that they are cat lovers. Some use special phrases which are understood best by "cat people".
==Description==
One aspect of cat culture is to wear clothing that identifies the wearer as a cat person.<ref>''[[Winnipeg Free Press]]'', December 31, 2018 - [https://www.winnipegfreepress.com/our-communities/metro/New-store-is-the-cats-meow-503718861.html New store is the cat’s meow By: Eva Wasney]</ref> Some of them have a type of language. Some of the "cat puns" they use are ''meowvalous'' which means "marvelous", ''Caturday'' which means Saturday and ''pawsome'' which means awesome.<ref>Canidae.com, August 18, 2017 - [https://www.canidae.com/blog/2017/08/words-that-only-cat-people-understand/ Words That Only Cat People Understand By Julia Williams]</ref> Cat terms such as "Purrfect" have been used in article titles such as CBS Sacramento's article about two cats living alone in a Silicon Valley studio, "Purrfect Tenants: 2 Cats Live Alone In A Silicon Valley Studio For $1,500/Mo."<ref>CBS Sacramento - [https://sacramento.cbslocal.com/2019/01/14/purrfect-tenants-2-cats-live-alone-in-a-silicon-valley-studio-for-1500-mo/ Purrfect Tenants: 2 Cats Live Alone In A Silicon Valley Studio For $1,500/Mo.]</ref> The culture has flowed onto the internet, where it now flourishes.<ref>''[[CNN|CNN Edition]]'', December 19, 2016 - [https://edition.cnn.com/2016/12/19/health/cat-culture/index.html 'Mysterious power over humanity': How cats affect health By Alice Robb, Science of US]</ref> According to a 2013 article by ''[[Psychology Today]]'', self-identified cat people have more unusual and distinctive personality traits than dog people. The article also speculates that due to the nature of cats and their sensitive nature, some people of a similar nature would feel an affinity with cats: thus cat people would be more sensitive than dog people.<ref>''[[Psychology Today]]'', April 17, 2013 - [https://www.psychologytoday.com/au/blog/unique-everybody-else/201304/cat-people-are-more-distinctive-dog-people Cat People Are More Distinctive Than Dog People Scott A. McGreal MSc.]</ref>  

In Morocco, cats are so much a part of everyday life that they are found everywhere. An article by ''[[Morocco World News]]'' said that people visiting there could think that the Moroccan person to cat ratio is pretty close to 1:1. Many Moroccans are said to love cats and they are part of everyday life, and around the city there are piles of food and water trays left for the cats.<ref>''[[Morocco World News]], July 23, 2018 - [https://www.moroccoworldnews.com/2018/07/251003/photos-moroccos-cat-culture/ In Photos: Morocco’s Cat Culture ''By Renee Sang'']</ref> A film by Tim Delmastro about what has been described as ''"Japan's Crazy Cat Culture"'' features [[Abroad in Japan|Chris Broad]], a British YouTube personality. Broad made his way across the country where he documented the numerous strange and interesting cat-centric activities, including cat bars, cat temples, cat islands and cat cafes.<ref>''[[PR Newswire]]'', February 22, 2018 - [https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/new-documentary-turns-japanese-cats-into-feline-film-stars-300603083.html New Documentary Turns Japanese Cats Into Feline Film Stars]</ref>

==In action==
Around the world countries have cat festivals. Singapore is full of cat lovers and it has its cat cafes and a cat museum. It finally had its first cat fest in 2018.<ref>''Weekender'', June 28, 2018  - [https://weekender.com.sg/w/do/indulge-in-your-cat-obsession-at-singapores-first-ever-cat-festival/ Indulge In Your Cat Obsession At Singapore’s First Ever Cat Festival]</ref> There is now a convention in [[Pasadena, California|Pasadena]], [[California]] called "CatCon Worldwide".<ref>''[[The Guardian]]'', April 16, 2018 - [https://www.theguardian.com/lifeandstyle/2018/apr/16/claws-pop-culture-crazy-cat-lady-lonely-sexless-eccentric-stereotype-millennial-ailurophiles Claws out! Why pop culture clings to the crazy cat lady]</ref> In August that year, the "POP Cats Convention" in [[Austin, Texas|Austin]], [[Texas]] was being held on the 25th to 26th.<ref>[[Patch Media|Patch]], August 21, 2018 - [https://patch.com/texas/southaustin/time-meow-plan-attending-austin-pop-cats-convention Meow Is The Time To Plan On Attending Austin POP Cats Convention By Tony Cantu]</ref> There are media sites for cat lovers such as ''[[The Catnip Times]]'' and ''[[The Purrington Post]]''.<ref>''The Purrington Post'' - [https://www.thepurringtonpost.com/ Home]</ref><ref>''The Catnip Times - [https://www.thecatniptimes.com/ Home]</ref>

According to the article "'Mysterious power over humanity': How cats affect health" by Alice Robb, there is a cat culture that flourishes online.<ref>''[[CNN]], December 19, 2016 - [https://edition.cnn.com/2016/12/19/health/cat-culture/index.html '' 'Mysterious power over humanity': How cats affect health'', By Alice Robb, Science of US]</ref> There is a cat-themed cruise where cat lovers can meet people of the same type and have cat parties.<ref>''The Travel'', January 25, 2019 - [https://www.thetravel.com/25-of-the-strangest-cruises-people-will-regret-booking-instantly/ 25 Of The Strangest Cruises People Will Regret Booking Instantly by Marika Kazimierska]</ref> There is a game called ''[[Cat-Opoly]]'' which is based on the board game ''[[Monopoly (game)|Monopoly]]''. With this game, cat lovers can buy cats instead of properties.<ref>''[[Bustle (magazine)|Bustle]]'', April 12, 2018 - [https://www.bustle.com/p/cat-opoly-a-cat-themed-monopoly-game-is-so-adorable-that-you-might-not-be-able-to-finish-the-game-8755095 Cat-Opoly, A Cat-Themed Monopoly Game, Is So Adorable That You Might Not Be Able To Finish The Game By Megan Grant]</ref><ref>''[[Metro (British newspaper)|Metro]]'', December 18, 2015 - [https://metro.co.uk/2015/12/18/cat-opoly-is-like-monopoly-but-better-because-you-buy-cats-instead-of-houses-5573161/ Cat-Opoly is like Monopoly, but better because you buy cats instead of houses - Francesca Kentish]</ref><ref>''[[OK!]]'', January 2, 2018 - [https://www.ok.co.uk/celebrity-feature/1265299/cat-monopoly-is-now-a-thing-and-its-the-perfect-board-game-for-animal-lovers Cat Monopoly is now a thing - and it's the perfect board game for animal lovers By Miranda Knox]</ref>
