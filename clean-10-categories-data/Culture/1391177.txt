{{short description|Non-profit organization dedicated to promoting the arts}}
An '''arts council''' is a government or private [[non-profit organization]] dedicated to promoting the [[art]]s; mainly by funding local artists, awarding prizes, and organizing arts events. They often operate at [[Arms-length management organisation|arms-length]] from the government to prevent political interference in their decisions.
