'''Alai Balai''' is a cultural event held during the Navarathri festivities before [[Dassera]]. It showcases the life of people in State of [[Telangana]].<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.telanganastateinfo.com/alai-balai-bjp-live-2015/ |title=Alai Balai BJP Live 2015|publisher=TSI |date=2015-10-23 |accessdate=2015-10-23 |location=Hyderabad, India}}</ref> The festival aims to spread camaraderie and brotherhood among people.<ref>http://www.deccanchronicle.com/tabloid/hyderabad/they-came-danced-and-opinionated-179</ref>

==History==
The festival was started by [[Bandaru Dattatreya]], [[Governor of Himachal Pradesh]] and Former MP and Central Minister first at [[Nizam College]]. The event is held every year in Hyderabad on the occasion of Dasara festival.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.hindu.com/2010/10/19/stories/2010101960630400.htm |title=Andhra Pradesh / Hyderabad News : Leaders get together for â€˜alai balai' fete |publisher=The Hindu |date=2010-10-19 |accessdate=2012-10-24 |location=Chennai, India}}</ref>

The event is well attended by all sections and politicians of all regions. A typical ambience of the rural area in Telangana is created and is popular for the food festival for its traditional food delicacies.<ref>http://epaper.namasthetelangaana.com/Details.aspx?id=79110&boxid=250891872</ref><ref>{{cite news | url=http://www.thehindu.com/news/states/andhra-pradesh/t-issue-congress-td-faulted-for-lack-of-unity/article4032097.ece | location=Chennai, India | work=The Hindu | title='T' issue: Congress, TD faulted for lack of unity | date=2012-10-26 | access-date=28 October 2012 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121027133433/http://www.thehindu.com/news/states/andhra-pradesh/t-issue-congress-td-faulted-for-lack-of-unity/article4032097.ece | archive-date=27 October 2012 | url-status=dead }}</ref>
