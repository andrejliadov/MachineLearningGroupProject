The '''International Society for Cultural and Activity Research (ISCAR)''' was founded in 2002 by the merging of the International Society for [[Culture|Cultural]] Research and [[Activity Theory]] and the Conference for [[Social environment|Sociocultural]] Research.  It is focused on [[sociocultural theory]], and its application to practice.
