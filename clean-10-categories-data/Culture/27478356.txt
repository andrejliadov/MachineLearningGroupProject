'''Heritage Week''' ([[Irish language|Irish]]: '''Seachtain na hOidhreachta''') is an annual nationwide set of hundreds of events organized by the [[Heritage Council]] in [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]].<ref>[http://www.ireland.com/entertainment/art/heritage-week/267370 Heritage Week]{{Dead link|date=January 2020 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}, [http://www.ireland.com/ Ireland.com].</ref>

They are a celebration of Ireland's culture, history, gardens, and architecture that gives visitors free access to landmarks that are either usually pay to enter or not open to the public. The week's attractions usually includes tours, events and activities related to history and culture.

Heritage Week is part of the [[European Heritage Days]], a joint action between the [[Council of Europe]] and the [[European Commission]].<ref>[http://www.ehd.coe.int/  European Heritage Days: A joint action of the Council of Europe and the European Commission], [[Council of Europe]].</ref>
There is a volunteer programme associated with the organization of the events.<ref>[http://www.heritagecouncil.ie/news-press/media/press-releases/national-heritage-week-launches-volunteer-programme/ National Heritage Week launches volunteer programme] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101107081740/http://www.heritagecouncil.ie/news-press/media/press-releases/national-heritage-week-launches-volunteer-programme/ |date=7 November 2010 }}, [http://www.heritagecouncil.ie/ The Heritage Council], Ireland.</ref>

Heritage Week is usually the last week in August.<ref>[http://www.heritageweek.ie/en/intro/about_us_introduction.aspx Introduction] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120227200054/http://www.heritageweek.ie/en/intro/about_us_introduction.aspx |date=27 February 2012 }}, Heritage Week, Ireland.</ref>

Event guides are usually available in Tourist Offices, Libraries, Heritage Centres, Museums, Bus Stations and in many hotels. There are competitions run as part of the weeks activities and awards, nominated by the public, for the volunteers and organisations which put on the best events.<ref>http://www.heritageweek.ie/</ref>
