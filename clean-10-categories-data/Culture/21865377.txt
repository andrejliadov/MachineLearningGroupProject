'''''Abolishing The Borders From Below''''' was an [[anarchist magazine]] published by a [[Berlin]]-based collective since 2001.<ref>{{cite journal|url=http://www.afed.org.uk/org/issue70/review_abolishing_borders_from_below.html|title=A review of abolishing the borders from anarchist federation uk|accessdate=7 March 2009|issue=70|work=Organise!|publisher=Anarchist Federation|date=Summer 2008|url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081120014747/http://www.afed.org.uk/org/issue70/review_abolishing_borders_from_below.html|archivedate=20 November 2008}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url= http://littleblackcart.com/index.php?main_page=index&manufacturers_id=4|title=The little black cart about abolishing the borders|accessdate=7 March 2009|work= announcement|publisher=little black cart|date=February 2009}}</ref><ref>{{cite book|title=Shock and Awe|publisher=New Pacific Press|location=City|year=2004|isbn=0-9712546-0-5|page=[https://archive.org/details/shockawe00inst/page/164 164]|url-access=registration|url=https://archive.org/details/shockawe00inst/page/164}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://abb.hardcore.lt|title=abolishing the borders homepage|accessdate=7 March 2009|work=project homepage|publisher=Abolishing the borders collective|date=March 2009|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20070218090903/http://www.abb.hardcore.lt/|archivedate=18 February 2007|url-status=dead}}</ref> It was formed by a handful of [[Eastern Europe]]an and [[Post-Soviet states|ex-Soviet]] migrant [[anarchists]] and political and counter-cultural [[anti-authoritarian]]s.

The bi-monthly magazine was filled with articles from [[correspondents]] around Eastern Europe and consisted of information about different political and cultural processes and activities in the region seen, commented on and analysed from an anarchist perspective.

It was printed regularly and distributed around the globe by Eastern Europe contributors and by Active Distribution,<ref>{{cite book|title=We Are Everywhere|work=Verso| location=London|year=2003|isbn=1-85984-447-2|page=455}}</ref> from [[South Africa]] to the [[Philippines]]. The magazine folded in 2010.<ref>{{cite news|title=Abolishing Borders from Below *36 November 2010|url=http://www.activedistributionshop.org/shop/magazines-and-journals/1744-abolishing-borders-from-below-38-november-2010.html|accessdate=17 February 2017|work=Active Distribution}}</ref>
