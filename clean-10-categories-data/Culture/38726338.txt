'''Anubhuti coach ''' is a luxury [[LHB coach]] belonging to the [[Indian Railways]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/chandigarh/Luxe-Anubhuti-coaches-to-roll-out-from-Kapurthala/articleshow/18786408.cms|title=Luxe Anubhuti coaches to roll out from Kapurthala - Times of India|website=The Times of India}}</ref>  These coaches will progressively be introduced on the [[Shatabdi Express|Shatabdi]] and [[Rajdhani Express]] trains.<ref>{{cite news|title=Chandigarh Shatabdi to have first Anubhuti coach|url=http://articles.timesofindia.indiatimes.com/2013-03-03/chandigarh/37409751_1_modern-facilities-and-services-rajdhani-train-anubhuti|access-date=4 March 2013|newspaper=Times of India|date=March 3, 2013}}</ref>

==History==
The [[New Delhi–Chandigarh Shatabdi Express]] will have the first Anubhuti coach, followed by Jaipur Shatabdi.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/news/news-by-industry/transportation/railways/chandigarh-shatabdi-to-have-first-anubhuti-coach/articleshow/18775465.cms|title=Chandigarh Shatabdi to have first Anubhuti coach|date=3 March 2013|newspaper=The Economic Times}}</ref> All Shatabdi trains will have these and later [[Rajdhani Express]] will also have them. The [[Western Railway (India)|Western Railway]] received its first Anubhuti Rail Coach on 12 December, 2017 for its [[Mumbai Central–Ahmedabad Shatabdi Express]].<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.mid-day.com/articles/mumbai-ahmedabad-shatabdi-express-to-soon-get-anubhuti-luxury-coach/18818187|title=Mid-Day Mumbai|date=2017-12-12}}</ref>
The [[Central Railway (India)|Central Railway]] also running Shatabdi express with Anubhuti coach between Pune - Secunderabad from 25 December 2017 as [[Pune–Secunderabad Shatabdi Express]].

==Design==
It is a [[state of the art]] LHB coaches with 50 seating capacity, featuring [[Ergonomical|ergonomically]] designed cushioned seats, LCD screens, modular toilets and stylish interiors, announced in the [[Railway Budget]] of 2014 by UPA Government, are to be produced at the Rae Bareli coach factory. They are fitted with automatic doors, the interiors and lighting arrangements will be aesthetically designed to enhance the ambience.

==Cost==
It is estimated to cost Rs.2.80 crores to manufacture an Anubhuti coach at the, [[Modern Coach Factory, Raebareli]].
