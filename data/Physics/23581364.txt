The term '''microphysics''' refers to areas of physics that study phenomena that take place on the [[microscopic scale]] (length scales smaller than 1 mm), such as:

* [[Atomic physics]]
* [[Cloud physics]]
* [[Mesoscopic physics]]
* [[Molecular physics]]
* [[Nanotechnology]]
* [[Nuclear physics]]
* [[Particle physics]]
* [[Plasma physics]]
* [[Quantum mechanics]]
* [[String theory]]
