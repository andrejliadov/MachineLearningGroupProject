The '''Klopsteg Memorial Award''' is an annual prize given to a notable physicist in memory of [[Paul E. Klopsteg]]. Established in 1990, it is awarded by the [[American Association of Physics Teachers]].<ref name=AAPT>{{cite web | url=http://www.aapt.org/Programs/awards/klopsteg.cfm | title=Klopsteg Memorial Lecture | publisher=American Association of Physics Teachers | accessdate=2012-01-25}}</ref>

The Klopsteg Memorial Award recipient is asked to make a major presentation at an AAPT Summer Meeting on a topic of current significance suitable for non-specialists.
