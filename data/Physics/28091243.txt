'''''Nuclear Physics News, International''''' ({{ISSN|1050-6896}}) is a quarterly [[List of science magazines|science magazine]] covering research in [[nuclear physics]], published since 1990 by [[Taylor & Francis]].  It is the official magazine of the [[Nuclear Physics European Collaboration Committee]], an Expert Committee of the [[European Science Foundation]],<ref name=sam>{{cite news|title=Sample Issue|url=http://www.nupecc.org/npn/npn243.pdf|accessdate=1 May 2016|work=Nuclear Physics News}}</ref> which was also established in 1990. The magazine is based in [[Garching bei München]], [[Germany]].<ref name="sam"/>

The [[editor in chief]] is Gabriele-Elisabeth Körner ([[Technical University of Munich]]).
