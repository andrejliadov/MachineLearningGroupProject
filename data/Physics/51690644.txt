The '''Swiss Physical Society''' (SPS) (German: '''Schweizerische Physikalische Gesellschaft''' / SPG, French: '''Société Suisse de Physique''' / SSP) is a Swiss [[professional society]] promoting [[physics]] in [[Switzerland]]. It publishes a journal ''SPS Communications'' (German: ''SPG Mitteilungen'', French: ''Communications de la SSP''), as well as organizes several [[academic conference]]s, [[symposia]] and [[workshop]]s.
