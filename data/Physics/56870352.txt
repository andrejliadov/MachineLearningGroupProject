The '''Aryabhata Award''' or '''Aryabhatta Award''' is an annual award, presented to individuals with notable lifetime contributions in the field of [[astronautics]] and [[Aerospace engineering|aerospace technology]] in [[India]].<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.thehindubusinessline.com/news/science/astronautical-society-awards-space-scientists/article7347051.ece |title=Astronautical Society awards space scientists |publisher=thehindubusinessline.com |date=23 June 2015 |url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180609071900/https://www.thehindubusinessline.com/news/science/astronautical-society-awards-space-scientists/article7347051.ece |archivedate=9 June 2018 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://zeenews.india.com/news/science/drdo-chief-gets-prestigious-aryabhatta-award_851488.html |title=DRDO Chief gets prestigious Aryabhatta award |publisher=Zee News |date=28 May 2013 |url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180609071900/http://zeenews.india.com/news/science/drdo-chief-gets-prestigious-aryabhatta-award_851488.html |archivedate=9 June 2018 |df= }}</ref>

It was instituted by the Astronautical Society of India (ASI) ''(estd.1990)'',<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.iafastro.org/societes/astronautical-society-of-india/ |title=Astronautical Society of India |publisher=iafastro.org |url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180226211734/http://www.iafastro.org/societes/astronautical-society-of-india/ |archivedate=2018-02-26 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://vssc.gov.in/VSSC_V4/index.php/conferences/64-conference/1409-asi |title="Health Monitoring and Fault Detection In Aerospace Systems" (HMFD-2015) |publisher=vssc.gov.in |url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180318183057/http://vssc.gov.in/VSSC_V4/index.php/conferences/64-conference/1409-asi |archivedate=2018-03-18 |df= }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=https://mukundsathe.com/tag/astronautical-society-of-india/ |title=This Day in History (22-Oct-2008) – India’s first unmanned lunar mission, Chandrayaan-1, was launched |publisher=mukundsathe.com - This Day in History |url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20180609071900/https://mukundsathe.com/tag/astronautical-society-of-india/ |archivedate=2018-06-09 |df= }}</ref> an [[International Astronautical Federation|International Astronautical Federation's]] member ''(since 1958)''. The award usually is presented by [[Minister of State]] in [[Prime Minister's Office (India)|Prime Minister's Office]]. The award consists of a citation and Rs.One lakh in cash.

== History ==
The award is named after the fifth century [[Indian astronomy|Indian astronomer]] and [[mathematician]] [[Aryabhata]],<ref>{{cite web |url=https://www.thebetterindia.com/22896/things-to-know-aryabhata-indias-first-satellite/ |title=10 Things You Probably Didn’t Know About India’s First Satellite And The Man It Was Named After |publisher=thebetterindia.com |date=1 May 2015 |url-status=live|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170728085436/http://www.thebetterindia.com/22896/things-to-know-aryabhata-indias-first-satellite/ |archivedate=28 July 2017 |df= }}</ref> and in commemoration of the first [[List of Indian satellites|Indian satellite]] [[Aryabhata (satellite)|Aryabhata]] (launched 19 April 1975).
