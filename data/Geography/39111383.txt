The '''Halford Mackinder Professor of Geography''' established in 1971 is located at [[St Peter's College, Oxford|St Peter's College]] of the [[University of Oxford]]. The post is named after Sir [[Halford Mackinder]], the first Reader in the Department of Geography in Oxford, and an important figure in the early years of Geography as an academic subject in the United Kingdom.
