The '''''Cartographica''''' is an [[interdisciplinary]] [[peer-reviewed]] [[academic journal]] and the official publication of the [[Canadian Cartographic Association]],<ref name=home/> 
in affiliation with the [[International Cartographic Association]].<ref name=pubs>[http://icaci.org/publications/]</ref>
''Cartographica'' is published four times a year by the [[University of Toronto Press]].<ref name=home>{{Cite web |url=http://www.utpjournals.press/loi/cart |title=Cartographica |publisher=[[University of Toronto Press]] |access-date=2017-07-05}}</ref>
