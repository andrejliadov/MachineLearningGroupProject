The '''Örebro Mission''' ({{lang-sv|Örebromissionen}}) was a [[Protestantism|Protestant]] denomination in [[Sweden]]. It was founded in [[Örebro]] in 1892<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kurt-ulander.com/beteltarnsjocom/John_Ongman.htm|title=Vad är då en människa|author=Kenneth Hermansson|language=Swedish|accessdate=11 January 2015}}</ref> and was part of the [[Baptist Union of Sweden]] until 1936. In 1997, the denomination became part of the [[Evangelical Free Church in Sweden]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.alltombibeln.se/kristendomenshistoria/svenkyrk.htm|title=Den svenska kyrkan från 700-talet till I dag|publisher=Kristendomens historia|language=Swedish|accessdate=11 January 2015}}</ref>
