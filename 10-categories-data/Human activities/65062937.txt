'''Pacha Nobin Jomoh''' Born (1st November 1990) is an [[Indian people|Indian]] Karate Player.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.sentinelassam.com/north-east-india-news/arunachal-news/nobin-jomoh-selected-to-represent-india-in-world-karate-championship/|title=Nobin Jomoh Selected To Represent India In World Karate Championship|work=Sentinel Assam News }}</ref> and also Police Inspector from the state of [[Arunachal Pradesh]] <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://arunachal24.in/arunachal-cm-attends-46th-raising-day-of-arunachal-pradesh-police/|title=Arunachal CM attends 46th Raising Day of Arunachal Pradesh Police|work=Arunachal124}}</ref>

Mr. P Nobin Jomoh is currently serving the [[Arunachal Pradesh Police]] as Inspector [https://arunachal24.in/arunachal-cm-attends-46th-raising-day-of-arunachal-pradesh-police/]<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.arunachalpradesh.gov.in/wp-content/uploads/normal_gazette/1537423503_17.%20Normal%20Gazette.%20No%20-17,(%20Part-I),%201-.%202018.pdf|title=The Arunachal Pradesh Gazette|work=Sentinel www.arunachalpradesh.gov.in }}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|url=http://arunpol.nic.in/Recruitment/sports14/Web_Accepted_APST_Sports354.pdf|title=List of Accepted candidates of APST meritorious Sports person for the recruitment of various posts under Sports Quota against Civil Police, AAPBN and IRBN in Arunachal Pradesh Police|work=Arunachal Pradesh Police www.arunpol.nic.in/}}</ref>and also designated as the Secretary of Athlete Commission Karate Association of India KAI (National Sports Federation) <ref>{{Cite news|url=http://karateassociationofindia.info/athlete-commission.php|title=Athlete Commission |work=Karate Association of India}}</ref>

Mr. P Nobin Jomoh (Black Belt 5 Dan), a four-time Commonwealth Karate Championship medal winner, has so far won five gold medals in various international karate events.

He is the first from the Northeast to win a karate medal in the [[Commonwealth Karate Championships]], and the first karateka from Arunachal Pradesh to participate in the [[Karate World Championships|World Karate Championship]] conducted by the [[World Karate Federation]] WKF.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://www.telegraphindia.com/north-east/jomoh-to-lead-team/cid/345584|title=Jomoh to lead team |work=telegraphindia}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://nenow.in/north-east-news/arunachal-pradesh/arunachals-karate-champion-nobin-jomoh-calls-on-cm-khandu.html|title=Arunachal's karate champion Nobin Jomoh calls on CM Khandu|work=north-east-news www.nenow.in}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://arunachaltimes.in/index.php/2019/04/04/aka-selects-37-karatekas-for-kai-cship/|title=Nobin selected to represent India at US Open Karate C'ship|work=Arunachal Times}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://arunachaltimes.in/index.php/2019/01/23/jomoh-wins-gold-in-national-kickboxing-championship/|title=Jomoh wins gold in national kickboxing championship|work=Arunachal Times}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://arunachalobserver.org/2018/12/07/arunachalee-karatekas-inked-history-india-dr-chai/|title=Arunachalee karatekas inked history for India: Dr Chai|work=arunachalobserver}}</ref> <ref>{{Cite news|url=https://thedawnlitpost.com/karate-1-premier-league-karate-chammp-2017/|title=Nobin Jomoh to represent India|work=thedawnlitpost}}</ref>
