'''French Fab''' is an accreditation award created 2 October 2017 in order to federate French companies and to promote French industries throughout the world.<ref>{{in lang|fr}}[https://www.usinenouvelle.com/editorial/bruno-le-maire-accelere-la-french-fab.N594218 Bruno Le Maire accélère la French Fab]</ref>

The French government has been inspired by the [[French Tech]] created in 2013 to promote startups and French IT.<ref>{{in lang|fr}}[http://www.latribune.fr/entreprises-finance/industrie/bruno-le-maire-lance-la-french-fab-pour-relancer-l-industrie-752569.html Bruno Le Maire lance la French Fab pour relancer l'industrie]</ref>

== History ==
The award has been created 2 October 2017 by [[Bruno Le Maire]], [[List of Finance Ministers of France|Minister of Finance]].<ref>{{in lang|fr}}[http://www.lefigaro.fr/societes/2017/10/02/20005-20171002ARTFIG00312-la-french-fab-le-nouvel-etendard-des-entreprises.php La French Fab, le nouvel étendard des entreprises]</ref>
