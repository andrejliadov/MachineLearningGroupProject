{{short description|Quantity of fish caught in fishing or game killed in hunting}}
{{About|the term 'bag' in the context of fishing and hunting|the non-rigid container|Bag (disambiguation){{!}}Bag}}

A '''bag''', in the context of [[fishing]] and [[hunting]], is a quantity of [[fish]] caught or [[game (food)|game]] killed, normally given as number of animals. Laws can restrict the number of animals killed through [[bag limits]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Hunting & Fishing |url=https://hunterwithinme.com/ |publisher=Hunter Within Me |accessdate=21 July 2012 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=About bag and size limits, possession limits and boat limits|url=http://www.dpi.nsw.gov.au/fisheries/recreational/regulations/sw/sw-bag-and-size/about-bag-and-size-limits|publisher=NSW Department of Primary Industries|accessdate=21 July 2012}}</ref> The term is also often used as in compound words, e.g. hunting bag scheme or bag statistics.<ref>{{cite web|title=Nature conservation and sustainable hunting in the EU|url=http://ec.europa.eu/environment/nature/conservation/wildbirds/hunting/index_en.htm|publisher=European Commission|accessdate=21 July 2012}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Artemis: the information portal of huntable species in Europe|url=http://www.artemis-face.eu/|accessdate=21 July 2012}}</ref>
