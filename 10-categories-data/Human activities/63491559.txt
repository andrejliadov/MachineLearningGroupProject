'''''Shinsekai: Into the Depths''''' is an [[adventure game]] developed and published by [[Capcom]] for [[iOS]] and the [[Nintendo Switch]].

==Gameplay==
''Shinsekai: Into the Depths'' is a two-dimensional [[Sidescroller|side-scrolling]], [[adventure game]] taking place in three-dimensional scenes. The player character works his way through a series of underwater environments, each containing a mix of puzzle and combat encounters. To overcome these challenges, the player has access to several tools and mechanics.<ref name="Destructoid">{{cite web | url =https://www.destructoid.com/review-shinsekai-into-the-depths-567509.phtml | title =Review: Shinsekai: Into the Depths | last =Devore | first =Jordan | date =March 26, 2020 | website =[[Destructoid]] | publisher = | access-date =March 26, 2020 | quote = }}</ref>
