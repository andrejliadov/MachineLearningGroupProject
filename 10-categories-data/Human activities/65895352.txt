E.|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=puQMAQAAMAAJ&q=olumba+olumba|title="I Am that I Am": Leader Olumba Olumba Obu, Sole Spiritual Head, Brotherhood of the Cross and Star|date=1988|publisher=Brotherhood Press|language=en}}</ref> in human form<ref>{{Cite journal|last1=Sabar|first1=Galia|last2=Shragai|first2=Atalia|date=2008-08-01|title=Olumba Olumba in Israel: struggling on all fronts|url=https://doi.org/10.1080/14725840802223549|journal=African Identities|volume=6|issue=3|pages=201–225|doi=10.1080/14725840802223549|s2cid=143483603|issn=1472-5843}}</ref> and also was the founder of the [[Brotherhood of the Cross and Star]] an organization which has both been described as a cult<ref name=":1" /> and a new religion<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Mbon|first=Friday M.|date=1988|title=Brotherhood Thanatology|url=https://www.jstor.org/stable/30118477|journal=Archives de sciences sociales des religions|volume=33|issue=65.1|pages=161–171|jstor=30118477|pmid=11616606|issn=0335-5985}}</ref>. ==Early life and biography==
Olumba was born in 1918 in [[Cross River State|Cross River]] state which is the south-south region of Nigeria  inhabited predominantly by the minority tribes of Nigeria. Olumba throughout his life had no formal education.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Who is this man OOO|url=https://www.bcs-uk.org/centenary/who-is-this-man-ooo/|access-date=2020-11-21|website=Brotherhood of the Cross and Star|language=en-GB}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=THE OLUMBA OLUMBA OBU STORY|url=https://www.thenigerianvoice.com/amp/news/13623/the-olumba-olumba-obu-story.html|access-date=2020-11-21|website=www.thenigerianvoice.com}}</ref>

==Brotherhood of the Cross and Star== 
The organization has been described as a new religion and a cult.<ref name=":1">{{Cite web|title=My wasted years in Olumba Olumba Obu's Evil Brotherhood|url=https://newsexpressngr.com/news/562-My-wasted-years-in-Olumba-Olumba-Obus-Evil-Brotherhood|access-date=2020-11-21|website=News Express Nigeria Website|language=en}}</ref> It was formed in the year 1956. The doctrines of the organization includes reincarnation and belief in African traditional religion. In the year 2000,<ref>{{Cite web|date=2020-04-23|title=COVID-19: Science has failed, I'm the answer – Olumba-Olumba|url=https://www.sunnewsonline.com/covid-19-science-has-failed-im-the-answer-olumba-olumba/|access-date=2020-11-21|website=The Sun Nigeria|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Christian|first=Eastwood|date=2020-08-06|title=Olumba Olumba Obu hands over to son, to be coronated as 'King of Kings '|url=https://ab-tc.com/olumba-olumba-obu-hands-over-to-son-to-be-coronated-as-king-of-kings/|access-date=2020-11-21|website=ABTC|language=en-GB}}</ref> Olumba transferred leadership of the organization to his son named Rowland, who would eventually rename himself from Rowland to Olumba Olumba,<ref>{{Cite web|date=2018-12-09|title=Olumba Olumba faithful to witness sect centenary celebration|url=https://guardian.ng/news/olumba-olumba-faithful-to-witness-sect-centenary-celebration/|access-date=2020-11-21|website=The Guardian Nigeria News - Nigeria and World News|language=en-US}}</ref> the exact name his father bore.<ref>{{Cite web|date=2012-02-17|title=144 Virgins are for celibacy - Olumba Olumba Obu|url=https://www.vanguardngr.com/2012/02/144-virgins-are-for-celibacy-olumba-olumba-obu/|access-date=2020-11-21|website=Vanguard News|language=en-US}}</ref>

==Controversy on vitality status==
Olumba's vitality status has been a source of great controversy. The members of his spiritual organization claim he is immortal, alive and well,<ref>{{Cite web|last=Edem|first=Edem|date=August 6, 2020|title=Olumba Olumba Obu hands over to son, to be coronated as 'King of Kings"|url=https://dailypost.ng/2020/08/06/olumba-olumba-obu-hands-over-to-son-to-be-coronated-as-king-of-kings/|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=|website=Daily Post}}</ref> whereas reliable Nigerian sources claim him to have been dead since 2003<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|last=Roti|date=2012-11-23|title=Olumba Olumba Long Dead, Followers are Being Deceived – Former Avid Follower|url=https://www.informationnigeria.com/2012/11/olumba-olumba-long-dead-followers-are-being-deceived-former-avid-follower.html|access-date=2020-11-21|website=Information Nigeria|language=en-US}}</ref>, coincidentally,  Olumba's last public appearance was in the year 2003. Three years after transferring leadership of the organization to his son. The spokesman of the Brotherhood of the Cross and the Star; Mr.''Shepherd Archibong'' said; 
<center>{{quote|“The ancient of days is above death and never dies. He is alive and existing in the dual nature of spirit and human”</center>}}

==Family crisis== 
Olumba Olumba is the father of both ''Helen Ibum Udoh''(née Olumba) and ''Olumba Olumba Jr''. Both siblings had internal conflicts which affected the spiritual organization negatively and resorted in a split of the organization with those loyal to ''Helen Ibum Udoh'' following her & those loyal to Olumba Olumba jr following him.<ref>{{Cite web|last=Published|title=I've not given power of attorney to my sister — Olumba|url=https://punchng.com/ive-not-given-power-attorney-sister-olumba/|access-date=2020-11-21|website=Punch Newspapers|language=en-US}}</ref>
