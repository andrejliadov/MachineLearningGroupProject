'''Stroud General Hospital''' is a health facility in Trinity Road in [[Stroud]], [[Gloucestershire]], England. It is managed by [[Gloucestershire Care Services NHS Trust]].

==History==
The facility has its origins in the Stroud Dispensary which was established in a room at the Lamb Inn in Church Street in 1750.<ref name=na>{{cite web|url=https://discovery.nationalarchives.gov.uk/details/r/d71cd199-6b62-4b6c-a8e5-b99ddb1ff684|title=Stroud General Hospital|publisher=National Archives|accessdate=11 January 2020}}</ref> After the dispensary had moved to permanent facilities at the corner of Bedford Street and George Street in 1823, an extension to create a proper hospital was completed in 1835.<ref name=na/> The current facility at Trinity Road was officially opened by the [[Charles Ellicott|Bishop of Gloucester]] in December 1875.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.stroudlocalhistorysociety.org.uk/research/hospital-origins/|title=The origins of Stroud General Hospital c. 1750-1874|first=C G |last=Burcher |publisher=Stroud Local History Society|year= 1999|accessdate=11 January 2020}}</ref> The hospital joined the [[National Health Service]] in 1948<ref name=na/> and the maternity unit was competed in 1953.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.stroudlocalhistorysociety.org.uk/people-places/buildings/stroud-hospital/|title=Stroud’s New Hospital opened 1875|publisher=Stroud Local History Society|accessdate=11 January 2020}}</ref> In February 2012 NHS managers agreed to halt plans for the hospital to be run by a social enterprise after local residents mounted a legal challenge in the High Court.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/healthcare-network/2012/feb/10/plans-halted-nhs-services-social-enterprise|title=Plans to move NHS services to social enterprise halted|publisher=The Guardian|date=10 February 2012|accessdate=11 January 2020}}</ref> Parts of the hospital were refurbished in 2012<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/uk-england-gloucestershire-20298138|title=Stroud General Hospital undergoes 'major refurbishment'|publisher=BBC|date=13 November 2012|accessdate=11 January 2020}}</ref> and in 2018.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.gloucestershirelive.co.uk/news/gloucester-news/ward-closing-stroud-general-hospital-1818096|title=This is why a ward is closing at Stroud General Hospital|date=24 July 2018|publisher=Gloucestershire Live|accessdate=11 January 2020}}</ref>
