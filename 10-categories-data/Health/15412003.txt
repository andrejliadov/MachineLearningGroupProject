'''BeHealth''' is a [[Belgium|Belgian]] [[eHealth]] digital platform which provides digital access to all health information and applications through one portal site, on behalf of both [[healthcare]] [[health care provider|provider]]s and [[patient]]s. BeHealth has been created on 23 December 2004. It first aim is to interconnect multiple applications from a number of [[social security]] actors, but with the goal to provide broader services over time.
