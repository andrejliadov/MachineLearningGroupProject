'''Being a Man Festival''' ('''BAM''') is a UK-based festival which addresses the challenges and pressures of masculine identity in the 21st century. The festival was founded in 2014 by [[Jude Kelly]].<ref name="Guardian_131213">{{cite news | last = Brown | first = Mark | title = Southbank festival asks: what is it like to be a modern man? | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160403125634/http://www.theguardian.com/culture/2013/dec/13/festivals-gender | archive-date = 3 April 2016 | url = https://www.theguardian.com/culture/2013/dec/13/festivals-gender | work = [[The Guardian]] | date = 13 December 2013 | access-date = 23 November 2017}}</ref>

== BAM 2014 ==
Speakers at the inaugural festival included: [[Grayson Perry]] (artist), [[Ziauddin Yousafzai]] (father of the Nobel laureate [[Malala Yousafzai]]), [[Michael Kaufman (author)|Michael Kaufman]] (co-founder of the [[White Ribbon Campaign]])<ref name="Observer_020214">{{cite news | last = Adams | first = Tim | title = Being a Man festival explores what it means to be male | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20170329175203/https://www.theguardian.com/society/2014/feb/02/being-a-man-festival-masculinity | archive-date = 29 March 2017 | url = https://www.theguardian.com/society/2014/feb/02/being-a-man-festival-masculinity | work = [[The Observer]] | date = 2 February 2014 | access-date = 23 November 2017}}</ref> [[Jon Snow (journalist)|Jon Snow]] (journalist), [[Billy Bragg]] (singer), [[Nick Hornby]] (writer and lyricist), [[Charlie Condou]] (actor) and [[Hardeep Singh Kohli]] (broadcaster and writer).<ref name="Telegraph_030214">{{cite news | last = Merz | first = Theo | title = Iâ€™ve changed my mind about Being A Man | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160120091526/http://www.telegraph.co.uk/men/thinking-man/10614251/Ive-changed-my-mind-about-Being-A-Man.html | archive-date = 20 January 2016 | url = https://www.telegraph.co.uk/men/thinking-man/10614251/Ive-changed-my-mind-about-Being-A-Man.html | work = [[The Daily Telegraph|The Telegraph]] | date = 3 February 2014 | access-date = 23 November 2017}}</ref>

== BAM 2015 ==
