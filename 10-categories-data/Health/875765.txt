The '''Baghiyyatollah al-Azam Military Hospital''' ({{lang-fa|بیمارستان بقیةالله}}) is a [[military hospital]] in [[Mulla Sadra]] Street in [[Vanak]], [[Tehran]], [[Iran]]. The hospital has ties with the [[Baqiyatallah University of Medical Sciences]].
