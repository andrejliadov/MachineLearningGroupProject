''''s Lands Hospitaal''' is a hospital in [[Paramaribo]], [[Suriname]]. The hospital started as a [[military hospital]] when it was established in 1760.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131022031041/http://www.dewest-online.com/personal/debye/landshosp/page3.html|title=Lands Hospital|language=nl|website=Dagblad De West via Archive.org|access-date=10 September 2020}}</ref> In 1934, the hospital was transformed into a general hospital and renamed 's Lands Hospitaal. The hospital has specialized in [[neonatology]] and [[pediatrics]].<ref>{{cite news|url=https://dagbladdewest.com/2019/09/10/s-lands-hospitaal-viert-340-jarig-bestaan/|title=Dagblad De West|language=nl|access-date=10 September 2020}}</ref> In 2015, an [[Intensive Care Unit]] was opened.<ref>{{cite news|url=https://www.dbsuriname.com/2015/12/18/s-lands-hospitaal-heeft-nu-intensive-care/|title=â€™s Lands Hospitaal heeft nu Intensive Care|website=Dagblad Suriname|access-date=10 September 2020|language=nl}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.atlasofmutualheritage.nl/en/Plan-hospital-Paramaribo.10420?fbclid=IwAR2JUEyRJbYM2sQD9KRLA2RsSQiccH-JkF_0PatTQpAWdG2xUOe4WPOaIog|title=Plan of Hospital in Paramaribo|website=Atlas of Mutual Heritage|accessdate=October 12, 2020}}</ref>
