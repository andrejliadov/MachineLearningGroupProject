The '''3rd [[National Geographic Bee]]''' was held in [[Washington, D.C.]] on May 23, 1991, sponsored by the [[National Geographic Society]]. The final competition was moderated by ''[[Jeopardy!]]'' host [[Alex Trebek]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2013/05/130522-national-geography-bee-alex-trebek-2013-jeopardy/|title=Alex Trebek: On Hosting the National Geographic Bee|first=Melody|last=Kramer|work=National Geographic|date=May 21, 2013}}</ref> The winner was David Stillman of [[Craigmont, Idaho]], who won a $25,000 college scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://press.nationalgeographic.com/files/2016/03/National-Geographic-Bee-Champions-List.pdf|title=National Geographic Bee Champions|work=National Geographic|date=March 2016}}</ref> The 2nd-place winner, Carlos De La Fuente of [[Chandler, Arizona]], won a $15,000 scholarship. The 3rd-place winner, Eliot Brenner of [[Richmond, Virginia]], won a $10,000 scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.baltimoresun.com/1991-05-24/news/1991144025_1_geography-carlos-trebek|title=Idaho youngster, 14, knows his orography, wins geography quiz|work=[[The Baltimore Sun]]|date= May 24, 1991}}</ref>
