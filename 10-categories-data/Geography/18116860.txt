The '''Most Ancient European Towns Network''' is a [[working group]] of the oldest [[cities in Europe]]. It was founded in 1994, with the idea coming from the town of [[Argos, Peloponnese|Argos]] in Greece, presented to the [[European Union]]. The group exists to discuss issues such as archaeological research, tourism and incorporating monuments into urban planning.
