'''Stephen P. Rice''' is a geographer and academic. Since 2011, he has been Professor of River Science at [[Loughborough University]].

== Education and career ==
Rice holds a [[Doctor of Philosophy|PhD]] from the [[University of British Columbia]]. In 1995, he was appointed [[Lecturer]] in Physical Geography at the [[Loughborough University|University of Loughborough]]. After promotions to [[Senior lecturer|Senior Lecturer]] and [[Reader (academic rank)|Reader]], he was appointed Professor of River Science in 2011. Between 2016 and 2018, he was also Head of Loughborough's Department of Geography and Environment, and since 2018 he has been Associate Dean for Research in the School of Social Sciences.<ref>[https://www.lboro.ac.uk/departments/geography/staff/stephen-rice/ "Professor Stephen Rice"], ''Loughborough University''. Retrieved 22 July 2019.</ref>

* (Co-edited with [[André Roy (geographer)|André G. Roy]] and [[Bruce Rhoads|Bruce L. Rhoads]]) ''River Confluences, Tributaries and the Fluvial Network'', (John Wiley & Sons, 2008)..
