'''Seyed Kazem Alavipanah''' ([[Persian language|Persian]]: [[:fa:سید کاظم علوی پناه|سید کاظم علوی پناه]]; born 1958) is a [[professor]] of [[Remote sensing|Remote Sensing]] and [[Geographic information system|Geographic Information System]] ([[Geographic information system|GIS]]) at [[University of Tehran]], and current Head of Department of Remote Sensing and GIS of [[University of Tehran]].<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=http://dralavipanah.ir/|title=Personal Website of Prof. Dr. Seyed Kazem Alavipanah|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://rtis2.ut.ac.ir/cv/salavipa|title=Seyed Kazem Alavi Panah - Resume page - University of Tehran|last=|first=|date=|website=rtis2.ut.ac.ir|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-09-07}}</ref><ref name=":2">{{Cite web|url=https://profile.ut.ac.ir/en/~salavipa|title=Seyed Kazem Alavi Panah - Personal page - Research Profile.UT (University of Tehran)|last=|first=|date=|website=profile.ut.ac.ir|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-09-07}}</ref>

== Education and career ==
Prof. Seyed Kazem Alavipanah was born in 1958 in [[Abarkuh]], [[Yazd Province|Yazd]], [[Iran]]. In 1976, he was admitted to the College of Agriculture, [[Shiraz University]], in the field of [[pedology]], and received his BSc in 1983. He commenced his master's degree program in [[pedology]] at [[Tarbiat Modares University]], Tehran, in 1988 and graduated in 1991. He received a PhD, from a Belgian University in 1997, in Remote Sensing (RS) and Geographic Information System (GIS), minoring in pedology. He was a faculty member at the International Desert Research Center (IDRC), University of Tehran, but in 2002, he was moved to the Department of Remote Sensing and GIS, Faculty of Geography. He is now serving as a full professor at the Department of Remote Sensing and GIS, University of Tehran.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1" /><ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://geography.ut.ac.ir/en/|title=Faculty of Geography,University of Tehran.|last=|first=|date=|website=geography.ut.ac.ir|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2019-09-07}}</ref>

== Scientific career ==
Currently, he is a member of a number international scientific association, [[Food and Agriculture Organization]] of the [[United Nations]] ([[Food and Agriculture Organization|FAO]]), and an elect member of the Intergovernmental Technical Panel on Soils (ITPS), in charge of preparing World Soil Report and World Soil Charter (2013-2015).<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://www.fao.org/3/a-i5199e.pdf|title=Status of the World's Soil Resources "Main report"|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://ut.ac.ir/file/download/news/1536566205-ff.pdf|title=Cultural Patterns of Soil Understanding|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref> He has been endeavoring on a program called ‘Heat as an Indicator of World Intelligence’ and trying to patent it as a theory.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://dralavipanah.ir/cv/heat%20%20theory.pdf|title=Heat an Indicator of World Intelligence as Theory|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref>

He has received many international awards for his projects, papers, and contribution to RS Conferences. In 2004, he was selected as the Superior Researcher of University of Tehran; in addition, he was the Select [[Professor]] of [[University of Tehran]] in 2005 and 2015. He attained [[:fa:جایزه ملی علامه طباطبایی|Allameh Tabatabai Award]] from the Iran National Elites Foundation in 2014.<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://rouhani.ir/event.php?event_id=444|title=Allameh Tabatabai Award|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref>

He has published more than 190 papers in noted international journals and conferences. Additionally, he has authored and published the following books: Application of [[Remote sensing|Remote Sensing]] in the [[Earth science|Earth Sciences]], [[Information technology|Information Technology]] in the [[Earth science|Earth Sciences]], Fundamentals of Modern Remote Sensing, Remote Sensing of Soil Salinity, Remote Sensing and Geographic Information System, and Research Methodology in Remote Sensing. His book Thermal Remote Sensing was selected as Superior Academic Textbook and commended by Iran government; this book was also among University of Tehran's 80 Treasure Books in 2015.<ref name=":1" /><ref name=":2" /><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=d4m1QX4AAAAJ&hl=en|title=Seyed Kazem Alavipanah - Google Scholar Citations|website=scholar.google.com|access-date=2019-09-07}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Seyed_Kazem_Alavi_Panah|title=Seyed Kazem Alavi Panah {{!}} Doctor of Philosophy {{!}} University of Tehran, Tehran {{!}} UT {{!}} Department of Remote Sensing and GIS|website=ResearchGate|language=en|access-date=2019-09-07}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=http://press.ut.ac.ir/book_1807.html|title=University of Tehran Press (UTP)- Thermal Remote Sensing and its Application in the Earth Sciences|last=|first=|date=|website=|url-status=live|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=}}</ref>
