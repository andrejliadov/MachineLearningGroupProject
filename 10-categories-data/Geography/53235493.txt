The '''5th [[National Geographic Bee]]''' was held in [[Washington, D.C.]] on May 26, 1993, sponsored by the [[National Geographic Society]]. The final competition was moderated by [[Jeopardy!]] host [[Alex Trebek]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2013/05/130522-national-geography-bee-alex-trebek-2013-jeopardy/|title=Alex Trebek: On Hosting the National Geographic Bee|author=Melody Kramer|work=National Geographic}}</ref> The winner was Noel Erinjeri of [[Swartz Creek, Michigan]], who won a $25,000 college scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://press.nationalgeographic.com/files/2016/03/National-Geographic-Bee-Champions-List.pdf|title=National Geographic Bee Champions|work=National Geographic}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1993/06/05/news/05iht-topi_7.html|title=American Topics|author=Arthur Higbee|work=The New York Times}}</ref> The 2nd-place winner, Michael Ring of [[Milford, Massachusetts]], won a $15,000 scholarship. The 3rd-place winner, Jeffrey Hoppes of [[Lancaster, Pennsylvania]], won a $10,000 scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.apnewsarchive.com/1993/Michigan-Teen-ager-Wins-National-Geography-Bee-With-AM-Geography-Bee-List/id-ced9fa80ca94529ca54c99c9cb475a46|title=Michigan Teen-Ager Wins National Geography Bee with AM-Geography Bee-List|author=Cassandra Burrell|work=Associated Press}}</ref>
