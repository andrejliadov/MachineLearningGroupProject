'''Ethnogeography''' or '''Ethnic Geography''' (ἔθνος ''+'' γεωγραφία) is the scientific study of the geographic distribution of [[ethnic group]]s.<ref>{{GSEn|Этногеография|Ethnogeography}}</ref> Ethnogeography is related to [[geography]] in the broad sense by its study of the influence of human activity and of ethnic entities as a whole.<ref>A. Kochin (ed.) {{Google books|V__UAAAAMAAJ|Ethnic Geography and Cartography|page=19|keywords=Ethnogeography}}</ref>
