'''''Cartopedia''': The Ultimate World Reference Atlas'' was an [[atlas]] program originally published by [[Dorling Kindersley]] Multimedia<ref>{{cite news|url=https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9C0CE1D81339F93AA35752C0A960958260 |title=CD-ROM Atlases Shrug Off Details |last=Manes |first=Stephen |date=1996-01-09 |work=New York Times |accessdate=2009-02-20 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20121106183102/http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9C0CE1D81339F93AA35752C0A960958260 |archivedate=2012-11-06 |url-status=dead }}</ref> in 1995.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.schoollibraryjournal.com/article/CA152994.html |title=The New World (Part II) |last=Del Vecchio |first=Stephen |date=1998-05-01 |work=School Library Journal |publisher=Reed Business Information |accessdate=2009-02-20 |archivedate=2009-02-20 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/5ejA6MKcY?url=http://www.schoollibraryjournal.com/article/CA152994.html |url-status=dead }}</ref>  It featured interactive world maps and graphs and charts of international statistics, and served as a reference guide for students and [[geographer]]s.
