The '''8th [[National Geographic Bee]]''' was held in [[Washington, D.C.]] on May 29, 1996, sponsored by the [[National Geographic Society]]. The final competition was moderated by [[Jeopardy!]] host [[Alex Trebek]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2013/05/130522-national-geography-bee-alex-trebek-2013-jeopardy/|title=Alex Trebek: On Hosting the National Geographic Bee|author=Melody Kramer|work=National Geographic}}</ref> The winner was Seyi Fayanju of Henry B. Whitehorne Middle School in [[Verona, New Jersey]], who won a $25,000 college scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://press.nationalgeographic.com/files/2016/03/National-Geographic-Bee-Champions-List.pdf|title=National Geographic Bee Champions|work=National Geographic}}</ref> The 2nd-place winner,  Ryan Bean of [[Augusta, Maine]], won a $15,000 scholarship. The 3rd-place winner, Matthew Conway of [[El Reno, Oklahoma]], won a $10,000 scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/archive/lifestyle/1996/05/30/right-place-at-the-right-time/e7cd415a-d408-4ff7-a2a3-32bd696bddc0/|title=Right Place at the Right Time|author=Linton Weeks|work=The Washington Post}}</ref>
