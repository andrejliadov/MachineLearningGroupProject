The '''Church of Mediano''' is a 16th-century submerged [[church (building)|church]] located in [[Mediano]], a submerged municipality in [[La Fueva]], [[province of Huesca]], [[Spain]]. Both were submerged by [[Embalse de Mediano]].<ref>[http://www.lafueva.com/mediano-2.htm Mediano, Valle de la Fueva, Pirineo Aragonés] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110201003700/http://www.lafueva.com/mediano-2.htm |date=2011-02-01 }} {{in lang|es}}</ref>
