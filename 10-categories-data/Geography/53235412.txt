The '''6th [[National Geographic Bee]]''' was held in [[Washington, D.C.]] on May 25, 1994, sponsored by the [[National Geographic Society]]. The final competition was moderated by [[Jeopardy!]] host [[Alex Trebek]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2013/05/130522-national-geography-bee-alex-trebek-2013-jeopardy/|title=Alex Trebek: On Hosting the National Geographic Bee|author=Melody Kramer|work=National Geographic}}</ref> The winner was Anders Knospe of [[Bozeman, Montana]], who won a $25,000 college scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://press.nationalgeographic.com/files/2016/03/National-Geographic-Bee-Champions-List.pdf|title=National Geographic Bee Champions|work=National Geographic}}</ref> The 2nd-place winner, Michael Bebow of [[Metairie, Louisiana]], won a $15,000 scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://articles.baltimoresun.com/1994-05-26/news/1994146069_1_mount-airy-national-geography-bee-mcdonogh-school|title=Mount Airy boy was national geography bee finalist|work=The Baltimore Sun}}</ref> The 3rd-place winner, Jeorse Lund of [[Henderson, Nevada]], won a $10,000 scholarship.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.deseretnews.com/article/355274/TEENAGE-MONTANA-BOY-PROVES-HE-KNOWS-WORLD.html|title=Teenage Montana Boy Proves He Knows World|work=Deseret News}}</ref>
