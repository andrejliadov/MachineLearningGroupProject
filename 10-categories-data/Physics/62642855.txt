The '''Clifford Paterson Medal and Prize''' is awarded by the [[Institute of Physics]]. It was established in 1981 and named after [[Clifford Copland Paterson]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.iop.org/about/awards/early-career/paterson/page_38665.html |title=Clifford Paterson Medal and Prize |publisher=Institute of Physics |date= |accessdate=22 December 2019}}</ref> The prize is awarded each year for exceptional early career contributions to the application of physics in an industrial or commercial context. The medal is bronze and is accompanied by a prize of £1000 and a certificate.
