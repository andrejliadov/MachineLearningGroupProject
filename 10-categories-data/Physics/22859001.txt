In [[astrophysics]], the '''Biermann battery''' is a process by which a weak seed [[magnetic field]] can be generated from zero initial conditions.<ref>{{cite journal|bibcode=2008ApJ...688L..57X|title=The Biermann Battery in Cosmological MHD Simulations of Population III Star Formation|journal=The Astrophysical Journal Letters|volume=688|issue=2|pages=L57|author1=Xu|first1=Hao|last2=O'Shea|first2=Brian W.|last3=Collins|first3=David C.|last4=Norman|first4=Michael L.|last5=Li|first5=Hui|last6=Li|first6=Shengtai|year=2008|doi=10.1086/595617|arxiv = 0807.2647 }}</ref> The relative motion between [[electron]]s and [[ion]]s is driven by rotation. The process was discovered by [[Ludwig Biermann]] in 1950.<ref>{{cite journal|title=Uber den Ursprung der Magnetfelder auf Sternen und im interstellaren Raum (mit einem Anhang von A.
