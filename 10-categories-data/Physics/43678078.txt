The '''I. I. Rabi Award''', founded in 1983, is awarded annually by [[Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers|IEEE]].

: "The Rabi Award is to recognize outstanding contributions related to the fields of atomic and molecular frequency standards, and time transfer and dissemination."<ref name=soc/>

The award is named after [[Isidor Isaac Rabi]], Nobel Prize winner in 1944. He was the first recipient of the award, for his experimental and theoretical work on [[atomic beam resonance spectroscopy]].{{r|soc}}
