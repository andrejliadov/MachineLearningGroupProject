This is a '''list of the [[Freedom of the City|freemen]] of the [[City of Gloucester]]'''.<ref>[https://www.gloucestershire.gov.uk/media/12190/26-freemen-of-gloucester.pdf Freemen of Gloucester.] Gloucestershire Archives. Gloucester, January 2013.</ref>

==17th-century==
* 1653 - [[Robert Payne (Gloucester MP)|Robert Payne]], member of Parliament.
* 1658 - [[Ralph Wallis]], nonconformist pamphleteer, known as "the Cobler of Gloucester".

==18th-century==
* 1762 - [[Benjamin Hyett (freeman)|Benjamin Hyett]].<ref name=rip>Ripley, Peter, & John Jurica (Ed.) (1991) ''A Calendar of the Registers of the Freemen of the City of Gloucester 1641-1838''. [[Bristol and Gloucestershire Archaeological Society]]. p. 136. {{ISBN|0900197323}}</ref>
* 1782 - [[John Phillpotts (land agent)|John Phillpotts]], land agent.
* 1789 - [[George Worrall Counsel]], lawyer and antiquarian.<ref>Ripley & Jurica, p. 170.</ref>

==19th-century==
* 1813 - [[Benoni Hill]], publican.<ref>Ripley & Jurica, p. 208.</ref>
* 1835 - [[George Viner Ellis]], anatomist.<ref>Ripley & Jurica, p. 257.</ref>
