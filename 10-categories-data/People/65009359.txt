'''Toni Scullion''' is a [[Scottish people|Scottish]] [[computer science]] teacher. She was a 2019 Cyber Security [[teacher]] of the year.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|date=2019-11-14|title=Scottish Cyber Awards: Meet Toni Scullion, Teacher of the Year Nominee|url=https://digit.fyi/scottish-cyber-awards-toni-scullion/|access-date=2020-08-20|website=Digit|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|last=Clark|first=John-Paul|date=2019-11-01|title=West Lothian teacher picks up top title at national awards|url=https://www.dailyrecord.co.uk/news/west-lothian-teacher-picks-up-20767595|access-date=2020-08-20|website=Daily Record}}</ref> She founded dressCode.<ref>{{Cite web|title=Speaker Details: EduTech 2020|url=https://canongate.swoogo.com/EduTech2020/speaker/127328/toni-scullion|access-date=2020-08-20|website=canongate.swoogo.com|language=en-AG}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Toni Scullion: Founder of dressCode Computing Science teacher|url=https://www.sheinnovatesstories.com/toni-scullion-founder-of-dresscode-computing-science-teacher|access-date=2020-08-20|website=#sheinnovates stories|language=en-US}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|date=2019-11-18|title=Toni Scullion {{!}} dressCode|url=https://wearetechwomen.com/toni-scullion-dresscode/|access-date=2020-08-20|website=WeAreTechWomen - Supporting Women in Technology|language=en-GB}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|title=Teacher Toni Scullion launches dressCode for kids|url=https://www.edinburghnews.scotsman.com/education/teacher-toni-scullion-launches-dresscode-kids-253386|access-date=2020-08-20|website=www.edinburghnews.scotsman.com|language=en}}</ref>

She has taught computer science at [[St Kentigern's Academy]].<ref name=":0" />
