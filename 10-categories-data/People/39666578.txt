'''Elvana Gjata''' ({{IPA-sq|ɛlvana ɟata}}; born 3 February 1987), also known [[:wikt:mononym|mononymously]] as '''Elvana''', is an [[Albanians|Albanian]] singer, songwriter, composer, actress and entrepreneur, who has been recognised for embracing a variety of [[musical style]]s throughout her career.<ref>{{cite web |title=Elvana Gjata–Biografia |url=https://www.teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata |website=teksteshqip.com |accessdate=9 February 2020 |language=Albanian}}</ref> Before her professional career, she performed in diverse singing and dancing competitions as a child.

Born and raised in [[Tirana]], Elvana gained significant recognition in the [[Albanian language|Albanian-speaking world]] with the release of her two [[Elvana Gjata discography#Albums|studio albums]] ''Mamës'' (2007) and ''[[Afër dhe larg]]'' (2011) both of which were influenced by [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] and [[Dance-pop]] music. Her follow-up [[extended play]] ''[[3 (Elvana Gjata EP)|3]]'' (2018) incorporated more elements of contemporary [[Pop-rock music|Pop-rock]] and [[folk music|Balkan-folk]] music.

=== 2005–2012: Career and albums ===

Elvana began to dedicate herself to music when she was fourteen as she auditioned for the Albanian talent show Ethet e së premtes mbrëma.<ref name="Interview"/><ref>{{cite web |title=Kjo vajzë është sozia e Elvana Gjatës |url=https://gazetablic.com/kjo-vajze-eshte-sozia-e-elvana-gjates-foto/ |website=gazetablic.com |accessdate=28 December 2019 |language=Albanian}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Kur Elvana Gjata ishte pjesë e Ethet e së premtes |url=https://klankosova.tv/kur-elvana-gjata-ishte-pjese-e-ethet-e-se-premtes-video/ |website=klankosova.tv |accessdate=28 December 2019 |language=Albanian |date=30 August 2016}}</ref> After one year, she participated in [[Festivali i Këngës]] with the song "Pranë Teje" whom she dedicated to God.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Festivali i Këngës]] |title=Elvana GJATA – Me tana |url=https://festivali.rtsh.al/kenget-konkurruese/elvana-gjata-me-tana |website=festivali.rtsh.al |accessdate=28 December 2019 |language=Albanian |quote=Një vit më pas, Elvana merr pjesë për herë të parë në Festivalin e Këngës me këngën e shkruar nga vetë ajo, “Pranë teje”.}}</ref> In 2004, she released her first single "Të Kam Xhan" composed for her participation in [[Kënga Magjike]] by her future collaborator and manager [[Flori Mumajesi]]. She began working on her debut album ''Mamës'' shortly after.<ref name="Interview"/> Since then, she has participated two times in the aforementioned competition, including in 2007 and in 2009 placing third with the song "Ku Jeton Dashuria" in 2007, and second in 2009 with the song "Dhe Zemra Ndal".<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[TV Klan]] |title=KENGA MAGJIKE 2009 |url=http://tvklan.al/on_air.php?id=34 |website=tvklan.al |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20100301062423/http://tvklan.al/on_air.php?id=34 |archivedate=1 March 2010 |language=Albanian}}</ref>

In 2011, Elvana released her second studio album ''[[Afër dhe Larg (album)|Afër dhe Larg]]'' with fifteen tracks, including the [[:wikt:eponym|eponym]]ous single "[[Afër dhe Larg (song)|Afër dhe Larg]]".<ref>{{cite web |title=Elvana publikon albumin "Afër dhe larg" |url=https://telegrafi.com/elvana-publikon-albumin-afer-dhe-larg/ |website=telegrafi.com |language=Albanian |date=10 August 2011}}</ref> The single earned Elvana her first [[Balkan Music Awards]] for Best Albanian Song of the Year, Best Music Video in the Balkans and the Best Song in the Balkans.<ref name="Balkanika"/><ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Balkanika TV]] |title=Elvana Gjata |url=http://www.balkanmusicawards.com/artists/item/164-elvana-gjata |website=web.archive.org |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20130813115532/http://www.balkanmusicawards.com/artists/item/164-elvana-gjata |archivedate=13 August 2013 |language=English}}</ref> The same year, she won the [[Top Fest]]ival with the song "Me Ty", which she dedicated to her friend [[Elsina Hidersha]] who died in a car accident that year.<ref>{{cite web |publisher=[[Top Channel]] |title=Elvana Gjata triumfon në "Top Fest 8" |url=http://top-channel.tv/artikull.php?id=212426&ref=onews |website=top-channel.tv |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110610131244/http://top-channel.tv/artikull.php?id=212426&ref=onews |archivedate=10 June 2011 |language=Albanian}}</ref><ref name="panorama.com.al"/><ref>{{cite web |title=Elvana triumfon në "Top Fest 8" |url=https://telegrafi.com/elvana-triumfon-ne-top-fest-8/ |website=telegrafi.com |accessdate=28 December 2019 |language=Albanian |date=5 June 2011}}</ref>

=== 2013–2016 ===

In early 2013, Gjata recorded five acoustic live sessions. During the year, Gjata released four new singles: "Fake", "Fake ''(Remix)''", "Beso" (Believe) (featuring 2po2) and "1990" (featuring MC Kresha). The single "Fake" was penned by Gjata herself and she criticized the current issues in Albania.<ref>[http://teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata/lajme_5052.php Përgadit ‘fake 2’] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130827051006/http://teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata/lajme_5052.php |date=27 August 2013 }}. Teksteshqip.com. Retrieved on 13 December 2015.</ref> The song reached a lot of success on social media and was called "Top Dance Track" on Deezer beating famous artists such as [[David Guetta]], [[Swedish House Mafia]] and many others.<ref>[http://teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata/lajme_4222.php Bëhuni Gati Për “Fake”] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130906095538/http://teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata/lajme_4222.php |date=6 September 2013 }}. Teksteshqip.com. Retrieved on 13 December 2015.</ref> It also reached No. 12 on [[Deezer]]'s top tracks beating [[Jennifer Lopez]], [[Emeli Sande]] and [[Rihanna]]. She released a remix of the song later, in a collaboration with [[Flori Mumajesi]], KAOS and P.I.N.T.<ref>[http://teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata/lajme_4387.php Së Shpejti Versioni Remix Për Këngën “Fake”] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130906115148/http://teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata/lajme_4387.php |date=6 September 2013 }}. Teksteshqip.com. Retrieved on 13 December 2015.</ref>

Gjata was nominated for four awards at the Videofest Awards for her single "Beso" with 2Po2, in which she took home two awards. During summer, Gjata toured around the [[Albanian language|Albanian-speaking]] territories, as well as [[Switzerland]], Germany and the Scandinavian countries.<ref>[http://teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata/lajme_3813.php Koncerte Në Vendet Skandinave] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130906094250/http://teksteshqip.com/elvana-gjata/lajme_3813.php |date=6 September 2013 }}. Teksteshqip.com. Retrieved on 13 December 2015.</ref> 
 
In early 2014, Gjata released her single "Disco Disco" which reached number one on MyMusic TV's charts and was one of the most successful Albanian songs of 2014. Later, it was announced that Gjata was the face of Albtelecom. In the summer of 2014, Gjata released another single called "Puthe" (Kiss). The single was a commercial success in Albania and many social media users paid tribute to the single.

== Artistry ==

Elvana's musical work and fashion sense are widely covered by media throughout Albania and the Albanian-speaking World. Her music is often defined as [[Contemporary R&B|R&B]] and pop, however, she also has incorporated a variety of other [[music genre]]s into her music, including [[Electronic dance music|EDM]], [[Folk-pop|ethnic-pop]], [[dance-pop]], [[Funk Music|funk]], [[pop rock]] and [[Soul music|soul]].<ref name="R&B"/><ref>{{cite web |title=Elvana Gjata |url=http://topawards.top-channel.tv/index.php/artist/elvana-gjata/ |website=topawards.top-channel.tv |accessdate=28 December 2019 |language=Albanian |quote=Stili i muzikës së Elvanës përmban frymat R&B, Hip Hop dhe Pop.}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Elvana publikon "Forever Is Over" produced by David Guetta featured ne Vevo! |url=https://radionrg.al/elvana-publikon-forever-produced-david-guetta-featured-ne-vevo/ |website=radionrg.al |accessdate=4 January 2020 |language=Albanian |date=14 June 2017}}</ref> She has named Albanian female Musicians such as [[Vaçe Zela]], [[Parashqevi Simaku]], [[Aurela Gaçe]] and [[Nexhmije Pagarusha]] as her musical influences.<ref name="Nexhmije Pagarusha">{{cite web |title=Elvana Gjata uron Nexhmije Pagarushën për ditëlindjen e saj |url=https://lajmi.net/elvana-gjata-uron-nexhmije-pagarushen-per-ditelindjen-e-saj/ |website=lajmi.net |accessdate=28 December 2019 |language=Albanian |date=7 May 2019}}</ref> 

In regard to her fashion sense, several observers have commended her style for its captiousness and versatility.<ref>{{cite web |title=Elvana vishet e tëra me të bardha, shkëlqen me pamjen e saj |url=https://kosova.news/2019/showbiz/elvana-vishet-e-tera-me-te-bardha-shkelqen-me-pamjen-e-saj/24489/ |website=kosova.news |accessdate=27 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=14 October 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=10 veshjet më të bukura nga vajza e ditëlindjes, Elvana Gjata! |url=https://www.intv.al/10-veshjet-me-te-bukura-nga-vajza-e-ditelindjes-elvana-gjata-foto-86042 |website=intv.al |accessdate=27 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=3 February 2019}}</ref><ref>{{cite web |title=Elvana Gjata duket bukur edhe kur vishet e gjitha në të zeza |url=https://www.kosovarja-ks.com/elvana-gjata-duket-bukur-edhe-kur-vishet-e-gjitha-ne-te-zeza/ |website=kosovarja-ks.com |accessdate=27 February 2020 |language=Albanian |date=4 November 2019}}</ref> Around the time of the release of her second [[extended play]] ''[[3 (Elvana Gjata EP)|3]]'', many critics praised the singers's style, sound and musical material describing her as an "icon of fashion".<ref>{{cite web |title=Elvana Gjata e tregon edhe një herë se është ikona e stilit |url=https://gazetablic.com/elvana-gjata-e-tregon-edhe-nje-se-eshte-ikona-e-stilit/ |website=gazetablic.com |accessdate=27 February 2020 |language=Albanian}}</ref>
