'''Babblarna''' (''The Babblers'') are Swedish fictional characters used as children's language tools, created by illustrator and writer Annelie Tisell.<ref name=Kristallen/> The characters are inspired by professor Irene Johansson and the Karlstadmodel to train people with vocal, language and communication problems.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.hattenforlag.se/inspiration/hattens-produkter/sprakvaskor|title=Spr책kv채skor|website=hattenforlag.se|accessdate=28 July 2020}}</ref> The characters Babba, Bibbi, Bobbo, Dadda, Diddi and Doddo were created in the early 1980s. Babblarna's Youtube channel has over 600 million views.<ref>[https://goodluckkk11.live/?utm_campaign=QPF8euu28II5lw7O2iHhCidoSOXmw5oLxD6bwphw43U1&t=main7d_ci_s Babblarna teater] Retrieved 28 July 2020</ref>{{verification failed|date=December 2020}} It won a [[Kristallen|Kristallen award]] in 2018.<ref name=Kristallen>{{Cite web|url=https://www.dagensmedia.se/medier/rorligt/har-ar-alla-vinnarna-i-kristallen-6928303|title=H채r 채r alla vinnarna i Kristallen|website=Dagens Media|accessdate=28 July 2020}}</ref>

Babblarna also has channels for English, German, Norwegian and Finnish.<ref>{{cite web |title=Babblarna: Channels |url=https://www.youtube.com/user/FilmHatten/channels |website=YouTube |access-date=3 December 2020}}</ref>
