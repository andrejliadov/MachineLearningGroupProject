'''Larissa de Macedo Machado''' (born March 30, 1993),<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.dicionariompb.com.br/anitta|title=Perfil: Anitta|publisher=[[Dicionário Cravo Albin da Música Popular Brasileira]]|language=pt|accessdate=March 31, 2018}}</ref> known professionally by her [[stage name ]] '''Anitta''', is a Brazilian singer, songwriter, television host, actress, and businesswoman.<ref>{{Cite news|title=Até o cocô! Veja se você tem uma amizade como Anitta e Nego do Borel|work=Uol|url=https://www.uol.com.br/splash/noticias/2020/10/22/ate-o-coco-veja-se-voce-tem-uma-amizade-como-anitta-e-nego-do-borel.htm}}</ref><ref>{{Cite news|title=Anitta desabafa: "No Brasil esperam o ídolo morrer pra falar bem"|work=Uol|url=https://www.uol.com.br/splash/noticias/ooops/2020/10/17/anitta-desabafa-no-brasil-esperam-o-idolo-morrer-pra-falar-bem.htm}}</ref> In 2010, after posting a video on [[YouTube]], Renato Azevedo, then producer of the independent record company Furacão 2000, called her to sign a contract with the label. Due to the success of the song "Meiga e Abusada" in 2012, she signed a contract with [[Warner Music Group|Warner Music Brasil]] the following year.<ref>{{Cite news|title=Nego do Borel e Anitta rebatem críticas após fotos sensuais: "Duda sabe que somos irmãos"|work=gauchazh|url=https://gauchazh.clicrbs.com.br/donna/gente/noticia/2020/10/nego-do-borel-e-anitta-rebatem-criticas-apos-fotos-sensuais-duda-sabe-que-somos-irmaos-ckgktndyo001h015xgmsyb46t.html}}</ref>

Anitta rose to national fame in 2013 after releasing the single "[[Show das Poderosas]]".<ref name="eonline">{{cite web|first=Diana|last=Marti|url=http://www.eonline.com/news/785793/5-things-to-know-about-anitta-olympic-opening-ceremony-performer|title=5 Things To Know About Anitta, Olympic Opening Ceremony Performer|date=August 6, 2016|accessdate=March 31, 2018|publisher=[[E! ''[[Ritmo Perfeito]]'' (2014), her second studio album, sold 45,000 copies after a month of its release.<ref name=meulugar>{{Cite web|title=ANITTA FESTEJA DISCO DE OURO POR RITMO PERFEITO|url=http://ego.globo.com/famosos/noticia/2014/08/anitta-festeja-disco-de-ouro-e-dvd-de-platina-realizacao-de-um-sonho.html|work=EGO|access-date=October 11, 2014}}</ref> On the same day she also released her first live album, ''[[Meu Lugar]]''.<ref name=meulugar/><ref>{{Cite web|title= Anitta alcança topo do iTunes com seu primeiro álbum de vídeo "Meu Lugar"|url = http://portalpopline.com.br/anitta-alcanca-topo-do-itunes-com-cobertor-e-2o-lugar-com-na-batida|work=Portal Pop Line|date = May 20, 2014|access-date=October 11, 2016}}</ref> In November 2014, she performed at the [[Latin Grammy Award]].<ref name="yn">{{cite news|title=2016 Rio Olympics daily viewer's guide - The Opening Ceremony (Aug. 5)|url=https://sports.yahoo.com/news/2016-rio-olympics-daily-viewers-guide-the-opening-ceremony-august-5-091444329.html|work=[[Yahoo News]]|accessdate=August 6, 2016}}</ref> In 2015, she released her third studio album entitled ''[[Bang (Anitta album)|Bang]]'', which was certified platinum and produced the singles "[[Deixa Ele Sofrer]]", "[[Bang (Anitta song)|Bang]]", "Essa Mina é Louca" and "Cravo e Canela".

In 2013, Anitta was the singer who most remained at the top of iTunes in Brazil and was elected by the same as the Artist of the Year.<ref name="eonline"/> She was also elected by the [[Associação Paulista de Críticos de Arte]] (APCA) as the revelation of the year in music in 2013. ==Early life==
Anitta was born Larissa de Macedo Machado in [[Rio de Janeiro]],<ref name="auto"/><ref name="auto1"/> the youngest daughter of Míriam Macedo and Mauro Machado;<ref name="family">{{cite web|title=Com casa cheia, Anitta lança o DVD 'Meu lugar' em show no Rio|url=http://ego.globo.com/noite/noticia/2014/07/anitta-faz-lancamento-de-dvd-em-show-lotado-no-rio.html|publisher=[[Rede Globo]]|accessdate=August 6, 2016|language=pt-BR}}</ref> her brother Renan Machado serves as her artistic producer. Her mother raised the two children by herself.<ref name="brother">{{cite web|title=Mães lindas de Anitta e Arthur Aguiar roubam a cena; veja quem está no 'Tamanho Família' de 24/7|url=http://gshow.globo.com/Bastidores/noticia/2016/07/maes-lindas-de-anitta-e-arthur-aguiar-roubam-cena-veja-quem-esta-no-tamanho-familia-de-247.html|publisher=[[Rede Globo]]|accessdate=August 6, 2016|language=pt-BR}}</ref><ref name="sdp">{{cite web|title=Anitta comemora os 10 milhões de visualizações de 'Show das poderosas': 'Amo muito'|url=http://extra.globo.com/tv-e-lazer/musica/anitta-comemora-os-10-milhoes-de-visualizacoes-de-show-das-poderosas-amo-muito-8545131.html|accessdate=August 6, 2016|language=pt-BR}}</ref>

She began her career at 8 years old singing in the choir of Santa Luzia Church in Rio de Janeiro by the intervention of her maternal grandparents.<ref name="auto"/> At 11 years old, with the allowance she got, she decided to take English classes. She later received dance lessons provided by her mother's teacher. At 16, she finished a course in administration at a technical school in Rio de Janeiro.<ref name="auto1"/><ref name="church">{{cite web|url=https://extra.globo.com/famosos/anitta-primeiro-beijo-emocao-com-telefonema-de-ivete-outras-historias-contadas-no-bairro-onde-ela-cresceu-8938380.html|date=2013-07-07|title=Anitta: O primeiro beijo, a emoção com o telefonema de Ivete e outras histórias contadas no bairro onde ela cresceu|publisher=[[Globo.com]]}}</ref> A year later, she decided to pursue an artistic career. That same year, she was the winner of the "Revelation of Music" award.<ref name="award and arts">{{cite web|title=Cantora Anitta é eleita a revelação do ano na música pela APCA - Notícias - UOL Entretenimento|url=http://entretenimento.uol.com.br/noticias/redacao/2013/12/10/cantora-anitta-e-eleita-a-revelacao-do-ano-pela-apca.htm|accessdate=August 6, 2016|language=pt-BR}}</ref>

Anitta's stage name was inspired by the character Anita, from [[Rede Globo]] TV series ''[[Presença de Anita]]''.<ref name="auto"/> She thought the character "amazing" because "[she] could be sexy without looking vulgar, girl and woman at the same times".<ref name="auto"/><ref name="auto2"/>

==Career==
===2012–2013: Debut album and initial success===
[[File:MC Anitta.jpg|Anitta in June 2013|150px|thumb|right]]
In 2010, Anitta was invited by [[funk carioca]] producer Renato Azevedo (known as Batutinha) to perform some tests after he saw one of her videos singing. After being approved, she signed with independent recording label Furacão 2000. That same year she released her first song on radio stations in Rio de Janeiro, the promotional single "Eu Vou Ficar", which was included on the ''Armagedom'' DVD, released by Furacão 2000. Advised by her producer, she decided to put another "t" in the stage name.<ref name="auto1"/><ref name="song1">{{cite web|title=Anitta, a preparada da vez|date=July 21, 2013|url=http://oglobo.globo.com/cultura/anitta-preparada-da-vez-9110966|publisher=Globo.com|accessdate=August 6, 2016|language=pt-BR}}</ref> A year later, the song "Fica Só Olhando" was included on the second version of the DVD.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://imusica.com.br/album.aspx?id=354463|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130917070137/http://imusica.com.br/album.aspx?id=354463|url-status=dead|archive-date=September 17, 2013|title=Armagedon II (Ao Vivo)|publisher=iMúsica|date=March 7, 2017}}</ref>

Her first televised appearance was on May 16, 2012 on TV program ''Cante se Puder'', on [[Sistema Brasileiro de Televisão|SBT]], where she sang "Exttravasa", a song by [[Claudia Leitte]], inside a beer cup.<ref>{{cite web|last=Paulo Dell Santo|first=João|title=Fast food vira tema de provas do "Cante se Puder"|url=https://rd1.com.br/fast-food-vira-tema-de-provas-do-cante-se-puder/|work=RD1|accessdate=February 25, 2019|date=May 15, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170528040223/https://rd1.com.br/fast-food-vira-tema-de-provas-do-cante-se-puder/|archive-date=May 28, 2017}}</ref> In June 2012, Kamilla Fialho, after watching a performance on stage by Anitta, offered to be her manager, paying a fine of $46.000 required to release the singer from her contract with Furacão 2000, later signing her to Kamilas's [[K2L Entertainment|company K2L]].<ref name="oglobo1">{{cite web|last=Essinger|first=Silvio|title=Anitta, a preparada da vez|url=http://oglobo.globo.com/cultura/anitta-preparada-da-vez-9110966|work=[[O Globo]]|publisher=Globo.com|accessdate=September 1, 2013|date=July 21, 2013}}</ref>

In early 2013, she created the "quadradinho" choreography, which was very successful among funk groups. In January 2013, due to the success of the song "[[Meiga e Abusada (song)|Meiga e Abusada]]" in Rio de Janeiro, Anitta signed a recording contract with [[Warner Music Group]].<ref name="auto1"/><ref name="song1"/> "Meiga e Abusada" became one of the most requested songs in Brazilian radio stations in early 2013. A music video for the song was recorded in Las Vegas, and directed by American director [[Blake Farber]], who has worked with [[Beyoncé]].<ref name="song1"/> Her debut, [[Anitta (album)|self-titled debut album]] was released in June 2013.<ref name="1stalbum">{{cite web|title='Mulheres estão tomando lugar dos homens no funk', diz MC Anitta|url=http://oglobo.globo.com/megazine/mulheres-estao-tomando-lugar-dos-homens-no-funk-diz-mc-anitta-8037896|accessdate=August 6, 2016|language=pt-BR|date=April 17, 2013}}</ref>

The music video for the song "[[Show das Poderosas]]" received considerable attention by the media in May 2013, becoming the most watched music video on [[YouTube]] in Brazil, surpassing 130 million views. Also helping to music, which remained for weeks at the top of the best selling in [[iTunes|iTunes Brazil]], and making it the third most played song on the radio in the country in 2013. The song also held the top ten of the music charts in Spain, Portugal and Argentina.<ref name="sdp"/> At the time, the singer's booking was estimated at $27.000 per concert, being one of the highest in Brazil.<ref name="1stalbum"/> In 2019, the booking of her concerts was $65,000. The singer does an average of 200 concerts a year.

=== 2020–present: Television and ''Girl from Rio'' ===
In January 2020, she released the single "Jogação" with the band Psirico. The music video for the song was recorded live during "Ensaio da Anitta", a show that took place at [[Triple Frontier|Espaço das Américas]], in São Paulo. The following month, the singer released the song "Rave de Favela" in partnership with the Brazilian singer MC Lan and the American group [[Major Lazer]]. Anitta also participated in the songs "Contando Lunares" by Spanish singer Don Patricio, "Joga Sua Potranca" by Brazilian DJ Gabriel do Borel and "Dança Assim" by Angolan musician Preto Show. Still in February, the singer participated in four episodes of the soap opera [[Amor de Mãe]] on [[Rede Globo]], playing Sabrina, a fan of the character Ryan (Thiago Martins), who ends up getting involved with him. During the social isolation due to the [[pandemic of COVID-19]], Anitta premiered in May directly from her home the program Anitta Dentro da Casinha, broadcast by the channel [[Multishow]]. Anitta prepares her first album all in English (the fifth overall), after the trilingual bet on [[Kisses (album)|Kisses]] (2019). The singer confirmed that she has thirty songs ready and is currently selecting the tracks that will make her album, along with American businessman Brandon Silverstein. [[Ryan Tedder]], lead singer of the band [[OneRepublic]] worked on the executive production of the disc, he is known for having worked on songs with [[Adele]], [[Beyoncé]], [[Bruno Mars]], among others. The information on the new album had been circulated on the internet in a post in the singer's biography on the website of S10 Entertainment, Silverstein's company, but after the news spread to the singer's fans the information was removed from the site.

In August 2020, Anitta released her first single in Italy with [[Fred De Palma|Fred de Palma]], "Paloma" which became one of the most shamaz'ed songs of the month in the country. In August 2020 she was also featured on WC No Beat's album on a track called "Cena de Novela" alongside Djonga & PK and also featured on the trap song "Tá Com o Papato" of Papatinho with Dfideliz & BIN.<ref>{{Cite web|title=TOP 10 ITALYA - Shazam|url=https://www.shazam.com/charts/top-200/italy|website=Top Shazam Italy}}</ref>
