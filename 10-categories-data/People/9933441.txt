This is a list of notable singers who have performed in the [[Afrikaans]] language. Solo artists are alphabetised by their stage name or surname—whichever is more common. [[Choir]]s that sing in Afrikaans are also included in the list, but other music groups are listed in the "[[#Music groups|Music groups]]" section below.

The first major South African singers to record in Afrikaans were [[Ada Forrest]] and Annie Visser, in 1908.<ref>{{cite encyclopedia |title= |encyclopedia=[[Standard Encyclopedia of Southern Africa]] |volume=5 |edition=1st |page=303 |year=1972 |publisher=Nasou |location=Cape Town |isbn=0625003217 |oclc=174335956 |quote=Apparently Annie Visser and Ada Forrest were the first major South African musicians whose work included recordings in 'the Taal' (early Afrikaans), both in 1908 in London.}}</ref>
