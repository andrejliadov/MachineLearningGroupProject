'''Omar Abdalla Aboelazm''' (born 25 May 1980) is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] citizen who was arrested in [[Denmark]] in 2010 over a [[2010 Copenhagen terror plot|suspected terror plot]] against the Danish newspaper ''[[Jyllands-Posten]]''.<ref name="dn_20101231_0842">{{cite news |title=Här är svenskarna som häktats |url=http://www.dn.se/nyheter/sverige/har-ar-svenskarna-som-haktats |newspaper=[[Dagens Nyheter]] |date=31 December 2010 |accessdate=31 December 2010 |language=Swedish}}</ref>

Aboelazm was born in [[Äppelbo]], [[Vansbro Municipality]], [[Dalarna County]], [[Sweden]], to a Swedish mother and an [[Egypt]]ian father.<ref name="dn_20101231_0842"/><ref name="svd_20101231_0908">{{cite news |title=Vad gäller saken? |url=http://www.svd.se/nyheter/inrikes/vad-galler-saken_5839605.svd |newspaper=[[Svenska Dagbladet]] |date=31 December 2010 |accessdate=31 December 2010 |language=Swedish}}</ref><ref name="expressen_20101231_1330">{{cite news |first1=Johanna |last1=Ekström |first2=Jessica |last2=Josefsson |first3=Arne |last3=Lapidus |title=Så blev Omar en misstänkt terrorist |url=http://www.expressen.se/nyheter/1.2273673/sa-blev-omar-en-misstankt-terrorist |newspaper=[[Expressen]] |date=31 December 2010 |accessdate=31 December 2010 |language=Swedish |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120205074022/http://www.expressen.se/nyheter/1.2273673/sa-blev-omar-en-misstankt-terrorist |archive-date=5 February 2012 |url-status=dead }}</ref> He has two half-siblings on his mother's side and five half-siblings on his father's side.<ref name="svd_20101231_0908"/> One of his half-sisters is a neighbor to [[Sahbi Zalouti]], one of the other suspects who was arrested in the same case.<ref name="svd_20101231_0908"/> He is unmarried and has no children.<ref name="svd_20101231_0908"/>

According to an article in ''[[Expressen]]'', Aboelazm has [[Asperger syndrome]].<ref name="expressen_20101231_1330"/> In 2000 he was sentenced to psychiatric treatment after an assault in 1999 when he also made sexual advances against a 25-year-old woman.<ref name="dn_20101230_1521">{{cite news |first1=Anders |last1=Forsström |first2=Clas |last2=Svahn |title=Terroristhäktade tidigare dömda för vålds- och sexualbrott |url=http://www.dn.se/nyheter/sverige/en-av-de-terrormisstankta-kand-sedan-tidigare |newspaper=[[Dagens Nyheter]] |date=30 December 2010 |accessdate=30 December 2010 |language=Swedish}}</ref> He had then also molested a 23-year-old woman, a 16-year-old girl, a 13-year-old girl and three 15-year-old girls on six occasions.<ref name="dn_20101230_1521"/> During a psychiatric examination in 2002, doctors concluded that Aboelazm suffered from a "grave mental disorder" and was in need of "psychiatric treatment associated with deprivation of freedom or other coercion".<ref name="dn_20101230_1521"/>

While detained in Denmark he was suspected of planned new attacks as police found instructions to construct weaponry in his cell along with plans of public places.<ref>{{Cite news|url=https://ekstrabladet.dk/112/article4038157.ece|title=Anklager: Planlagde ny terror fra cellen|access-date=2018-10-13|language=da}}</ref> After being transferred to Swedish prison, he received a number of warnings for refusing to work and disorderly conduct.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://doku.nu/2018/10/08/terroristen-forsokte-radikalisera-sina-medfangar/|title=Terroristen försökte radikalisera sina medfångar – Doku.nu|website=doku.nu|language=sv-SE|access-date=2018-10-13}}</ref>
