'''Miina Äkkijyrkkä''' aka '''Liina Lång''', originally Riitta Loiva (born July 2, 1949 in [[Iisalmi]], [[Finland]]) is a [[Finns|Finnish]] artist. She is known for her [[painting]]s, [[drawing]]s and [[sculpture]]s. She is also a protector of [[Finncattle]], the native Finnish [[dairy]] [[breed]]. She is known for her disdain for mainstream culture, and her actions have aroused controversy.<ref>[http://www.astrafilm.ro/en/cinematheque-2008/cinematheque-astra-film-sibiu-2008-women#7 ''Astra Film Sibiu: Miina!''  2007] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090426041216/http://www.astrafilm.ro/en/cinematheque-2008/cinematheque-astra-film-sibiu-2008-women |date=2009-04-26 }}</ref>

== Personal life ==
Äkkijyrkkä has three children.<ref>https://web.archive.org/web/20140714134309/http://www.akkijyrkka.com/miina/cvsuomi.pdf</ref> She stated in 2008 and 2009 that she is on the autism spectrum.<ref>Äkkijyrkkä's interview on Apu magazine 31/2008</ref><ref>TV show Levengood from Finland, broadcast July 7, 2009</ref>
