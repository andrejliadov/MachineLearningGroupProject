This '''bibliography of  Stanisław Lem''' is a list of works about [[Stanisław Lem]], a [[Poland|Polish]] [[science fiction]] writer and essayist.

In addition to books and numerous academic articles, Lem's works and ideas have been a subject of  a number of Ph.D. and master theses.<ref>[https://solaris.lem.pl/o-lemie/teksty-o-lemie/prace-dyplomowe  O Lemie  -> Teksty ->Prace dyplomowe]</ref>
