'''''Hope! – Das Obama Musical''''' ([[English language|English]]: ''Hope! - The Obama Musical'') is a [[Germany|German]] [[Musical theatre|musical]] inspired by the [[Early life and career of Barack Obama|personal life and career]] of [[President of the United States|U.S. President]] [[Barack Obama]], from his time in [[Chicago]], [[Illinois]], until the [[2008 United States presidential election|2008 U.S. presidential election]].<ref name=guardian>{{cite news | url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2010/jan/17/obama-musical-hope-frankfurt | title=Obama das musical makes a song and dance of US election | last=Connolly | first=Kate | date=January 17, 2010 | work=[[The Guardian]] | accessdate=January 18, 2010 }}</ref><ref name=npr>{{cite news | url=https://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=122613570 | title=Yes You Can — See The New Obama Musical | last=Raz | first=Guy | date=January 17, 2010 | work=[[All Things Considered]] | publisher=[[National Public Radio]] | accessdate=January 18, 2010 }}</ref>  The show premiered on January 17, 2010, at [[Frankfurt]]'s Jahrhunderthalle, a 2,000-seat venue.<ref name=wsj>{{cite news | url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB10001424052748704541004575010473563972754 | title=Obama Mia! | last=Schwammenthal | first=Daniel | date=January 19, 2010 | work=[[The Wall Street Journal]] | accessdate=January 18, 2010 }}</ref><ref name=tapper>{{cite news | url=http://blogs.abcnews.com/politicalpunch/2010/01/ich-bin-ein-hope-monger-obama-musical-premieres-in-germany.html | title=Ich Bin Ein Hope-Monger: Obama Musical Premieres in Germany | last=Tapper | first=Jake | author-link=Jake Tapper | date=January 18, 2010 | work=[[ABC News]] | accessdate=January 18, 2010 }}</ref>  It is believed to be the first stage production portraying the life of a U.S. president while he is still serving in office.<ref name=guardian/>
