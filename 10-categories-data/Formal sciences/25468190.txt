Antitrust/Competition; Commercial Damages; Electric Utilities; Environmental Litigation and Regulation; Forensic Economics; Intellectual Property; International Arbitration; International Trade; Product Liability; Regulatory Finance and Accounting; Risk Management; Securities; Tax; Utility Regulatory Policy and Ratemaking; Valuation

==Brattle Prize in Corporate Finance==
The company presents the [[Brattle Prize]] each year at the [[American Finance Association]]'s annual meeting. It is awarded for outstanding papers on corporate finance, published in the ''[[Journal of Finance]]''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.afajof.org/journal/edreport.asp|accessdate=2007-09-10|title=Journal of Finance Editor's Report|publisher=[[American Finance Association]]|date=2007-03-13|last=Harvey|first=Cam|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070812022836/http://www.afajof.org/journal/edreport.asp|archive-date=2007-08-12|url-status=dead}}</ref>
