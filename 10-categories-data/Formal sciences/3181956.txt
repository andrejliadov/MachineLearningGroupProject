'''[[Actuarial science]]''' &ndash; discipline that applies [[mathematics|mathematical]] and [[statistics|statistical]] methods to [[Risk assessment|assess risk]] in the [[insurance]] and [[finance]] industries.

{{TOC limit|limit=2}}

== What ''type'' of thing is actuarial science? ==
Actuarial science can be described as all of the following:

* An [[academic discipline]] &ndash; 
* A branch of [[science]] &ndash; 
** An [[applied science]] &ndash; 
*** A subdiscipline of [[statistics]] &ndash;
