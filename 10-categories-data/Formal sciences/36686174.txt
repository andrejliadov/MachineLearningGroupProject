'''''Decision Sciences Journal of Innovative Education''''' is a [[Peer review|peer-reviewed]] [[academic journal]] published by [[Wiley-Blackwell]] on behalf of the [[Decision Sciences Institute]]. The current [[editor-in-chief]] is [[Chetan Sankar]] ([[Auburn University]]). The  journal covers all areas related to [[decision science]].
