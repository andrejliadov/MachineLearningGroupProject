The '''Basel Computational Biology Conference''' (stylized as '''[BC]<sup>2</sup>''') is a scientific meeting on the subjects of [[bioinformatics]] and [[computational biology]]. It covers a wide spectrum of disciplines, including bioinformatics, computational biology, [[genomics]], computational [[structural biology]], and [[systems biology]]. The conference is organized biannually by the SIB [[Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics]] in Basel, Switzerland.
