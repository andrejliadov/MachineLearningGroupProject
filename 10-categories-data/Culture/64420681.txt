'''''Religious America''''' was a 1974 American television [[documentary series]] produced for the [[Public Broadcasting Service]] (PBS) television station [[WGBH-TV]] in Boston and covered various religious communities in the [[United States]]. The series producer was produced [[Philip Garvin]] and directed by [[Boyd Estus]] and Philip Garvin. The show included 13 weekly episodes.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1974/01/05/archives/tv-american-religious-experience-series-on-channel-13-starts.html|title=TV: American Religious Experience|first=John J.|last=O'Connor|date=January 5, 1974|via=NYTimes.com}}</ref><ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1974/01/06/archives/yes-there-are-people-out-there-who-believe-a-new-tv-series-finds-a.html|title=Yes, There Are People ‘Out There’ Who Believe|first=Charles M.|last=Austin|date=January 6, 1974|via=NYTimes.com}}</ref>
