'''AddToAny''' is a universal [[Sharing#In computer and Internet culture|sharing]] platform founded by [[Pat Diven II]] that can be integrated into a website by use of a [[web widget]] or [[Plug-in (computing)|plugin]]. Once installed, visitors to the website can share or save an item using a variety of services, such as [[Facebook]], [[Twitter]], [[Pinterest]], [[email]], and over 100 other services.<ref name=crunchbase />  AddToAny makes money by selling anonymous aggregate sharing data to advertisers.<ref name=venturebeat />

Official [[plugins]] are available for [[Open-source software|open source]] [[content management systems]] such as [[WordPress]], [[Drupal]] and [[Joomla]], and hosted content management systems such as [[WordPress.com]], [[Blogger (service)]], [[Tumblr]] and [[TypePad]]. Having received over 8.6&nbsp;million downloads, the AddToAny plugin is one of the most popular WordPress plugins and the most installed sharing plugin for WordPress.<ref>{{cite web |title=WordPress Plugins Directory |url=https://wordpress.org/plugins/browse/popular/ }}</ref> On Drupal, the AddToAny module is actively used on over 41,000 Drupal sites and is the most actively used sharing module.<ref>{{cite web |title=Usage statistics for AddToAny Share/Bookmark Button |url=https://drupal.org/project/usage/addtoany}}</ref>

AddToAny also provides browser extensions for end-users to share the web page they are browsing to any service.<ref name=":0" /><ref name=":1">{{Cite web|url=https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/addtoany-share/|title=AddToAny: Share Anywhere – Add-ons for Firefox|website=addons.mozilla.org|language=en-US}}</ref>

==History==
* March 2006, launched as the first all-inclusive [[web page]] sharing, [[Social bookmarking|bookmarking]], and [[web feed]] subscription tool.<ref name=crunchbase />
* 2009, launched the [[Google Chrome|Chrome]] extension.<ref name=":0">{{Cite web|url=https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/addtoany-share-anywhere/ffpgijchhhkhnokafdeklpllijgnbche|title=AddToAny: Share Anywhere|website=chrome.google.com|language=en|access-date=2017-03-20}}</ref>
* July 2011, acquired by [[social commerce]] site [[Lockerz]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2011/07/12/kleiner-backed-lockerz-acquires-social-sharing-platform-addtoany/ |title=Lockerz Acquires AddToAny|publisher=TechCrunch Profile}}</ref>
* June 2013, reacquired by [[Pat Diven II]], AddToAny's original founder.<ref name=venturebeat /><ref>{{cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2013/07/02/lockerzs-pivot-continues-as-it-sells-the-addtoany-link-sharing-tool-back-to-its-founder/ |title=Diven Reacquires AddToAny from Lockerz|publisher=TechCrunch}}</ref>
*November 2014, launched the [[Joomla]] extension.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://extensions.joomla.org/add-an-extension/extension/social-web/social-share/addtoany-share-buttons/|title=AddToAny Share Buttons, by AddToAny - Joomla Extension Directory|website=extensions.joomla.org|language=en-gb|access-date=2018-06-20}}</ref>
*March 2017, launched the [[Firefox]] extension.<ref name=":1" />
