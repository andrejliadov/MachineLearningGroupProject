'''Heroes of the Environment''' was a list of a year's most notable [[environmentalists]] chosen and compiled by ''[[Time (magazine)|Time]]'' magazine.<ref name="Aldred">{{cite news|last1=Aldred|first1=Jessica|title=Heroes of the environment|url=https://www.theguardian.com/environment/blog/2007/oct/22/heroesoftheenvironment|accessdate=26 April 2018|publisher=[[The Guardian]]|date=22 October 2007}}</ref><ref name="Walsh">{{cite news|last1=Walsh|first1=Bryan|title=Heroes of the Environment|url=http://content.time.com/time/specials/2007/article/0,28804,1663317_1672378,00.html|accessdate=26 April 2018|publisher=Time Magazine|date=17 October 2017}}</ref><ref name="MeisnerTakahashi">{{cite journal|last1=Meisner|first1=Mark|last2=Takahashi|first2=Bruno|title=The Nature of Time: How the Covers of the World's Most Widely Read Weekly News Magazine Visualize Environmental Affairs|journal=Environmental Communication|date=2017|volume=7|issue=2|pages=255–276|doi=10.1080/17524032.2013.772908}}</ref>

The award was established in 2007.
