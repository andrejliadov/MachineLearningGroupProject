A '''historical sovereign state''' is a state that once existed, but has since been dissolved due to conflict, war, rebellion, annexation, or uprising. This page lists [[sovereign states]], [[country|countries]], [[nation]]s, or [[empire]]s that have ceased to exist as political entities, grouped geographically and by constitutional nature. The asserted [[sovereignty]] of some states listed here was generally unrecognized.

{{Expand list|date=August 2008}}

==Criteria for inclusion==
The criteria for inclusion in this list is similar to that of the [[List of states with limited recognition]]. To be included here, a polity must have claimed statehood and either:
* had de facto control over a territory, a population, a government, a capacity to enter into relations with other states, or
* have been recognised as a state by at least one other state.

For purposes of this list, the cutoff between medieval and early modern states is the [[Fall of Constantinople]] in 1453.

==Modern states and territories by type==

===Dismembered countries===
These states are now dissolved into a number of states, none of which retain the old name.
*{{Flag|Nazi Germany|name=Greater German Reich}} – Dissolved in 1945, its former territory now consists of the entirety of the countries of [[Austria]] and [[Germany]] and parts of what is now [[Belarus]], the [[Czech Republic]], [[France]], [[Luxembourg]], [[Poland]], [[Russia]], [[Belgium]], the [[Netherlands]], [[Denmark]], [[Norway]], [[Greece]], [[Serbia]], [[Estonia]], [[Latvia]], [[Lithuania]], [[Ukraine]] and [[Slovenia]].
*{{Flagcountry|Korean Empire}} – Ceased to exist in 1910, its former territory now consists of the entirety of territory controlled by [[North Korea]] and [[South Korea]] and a portion of territory claimed by [[Japan]].
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Mali (1959-1961).svg}} [[Mali Federation]]&nbsp;– In 1959 formed by [[Senegal]] and [[French Sudan]], both parts of [[French West Africa]], as an independent nation. It fell apart in 1960 into [[Senegal]] and [[Mali]].
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of the United Kingdom of Portugal, Brazil, and Algarves.svg}} [[United Kingdom of Portugal, Brazil and the Algarves|Portugal, Brazil and the Algarves]] (''Reino Unido de Portugal, Brasil e Algarves'') created in 1815 when Brazil was upgraded to the rank of kingdom, once the Portuguese royal family was living in [[Rio de Janeiro]] since 1809. This country was dissolved in 1822 when Brazil became independent. Now the countries of Portugal, Brazil, Cabo Verde, São Tomé and Príncipe, Angola, East Timor and Mozambique.
*{{Flag|Serbia and Montenegro}} – Dissolved in 2006, now the countries of [[Kosovo]], [[Montenegro]], and [[Serbia]].
*{{Flag|Soviet Union}} – Dissolved in 1991, now the countries of [[Armenia]], [[Azerbaijan]], [[Belarus]], [[Estonia]], [[Georgia (country)|Georgia]], [[Kazakhstan]], [[Kyrgyzstan]], [[Latvia]], [[Lithuania]], [[Moldova]], [[Russia]], [[Tajikistan]], [[Turkmenistan]], [[Ukraine]], and [[Uzbekistan]].
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Syria.svg}} [[United Arab Republic]]&nbsp;– A union formed by [[Egypt]] and [[Syria]] in 1958. It was dissolved in 1961, though Egypt used the name until 1971. Other [[Pan-Arab]] unity agreements with [[Iraq]] and [[Jordan]] of the 1950s failed.
* [[United Arab States]] – A confederation formed by the [[United Arab Republic]] and [[Mutawakkilite Kingdom of Yemen|North Yemen]] in 1958; it was dissolved in 1961.
*{{Flagcountry|Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia}} – Dissolved in 1991 and 1992, now the countries of [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]], [[Croatia]], [[Kosovo]], [[Montenegro]], [[North Macedonia]], [[Serbia]], and [[Slovenia]].
*{{Flagcountry|Czechoslovakia}} - Was in power from 1918–1992, the country wasn't active in [[World War II]], but the government was active, dissolved in 1992 and broke up into [[Czech Republic|Czechia]] and [[Slovakia]].
*{{Flagcountry|Federal Republic of Central America}} - Was in power from 1821–1841, broke up into [[Guatemala]], [[Belize]], [[El Salvador]], [[Honduras]], [[Nicaragua]], and [[Free State of Costa Rica|Costa Rica]].

===Nominally-independent homelands of South Africa===
Four of the [[Homeland (South Africa)|homelands]], or [[bantustans]], for [[Black (racial term)|black]] South Africans, were granted nominal independence by the [[apartheid]] regime of [[South Africa]]. Not recognised by other nations, these effectively were [[puppet state]]s and were [[political union|re-incorporated]] in 1994.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Bophuthatswana (1972–1994).svg}} [[Bophuthatswana]]&nbsp;– Declared independent in 1977, reincorporated in 1994.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Ciskei.svg}} [[Ciskei]]&nbsp;– Declared independent in 1981, reincorporated in 1994.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Transkei.svg}} [[Transkei]]&nbsp;– Declared independent in 1976, reincorporated in 1994.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Venda (1973–1994).svg}} [[Venda]]&nbsp;– Declared independent in 1979, reincorporated in 1994.

===Secessionist states===
These nations declared themselves independent, but failed to achieve it in fact or did not seek permanent independence and were either re-incorporated into the mother country or incorporated into another country.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Ukraine.svg}} [[Carpatho-Ukraine]]&nbsp;– declared independence from [[Czechoslovakia]] in 1939, but was [[Annexation|annexed]] by Hungary in one day.
* [[Cartagena Canton]]&nbsp;– the haven city of [[Cartagena, Spain]] seceded from the [[First Spanish Republic]] in 1873.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Catalonia.svg}} [[Catalan Republic (1931)|Catalan Republic]] (April 14 – April 17, 1931).
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Chechen Republic of Ichkeria.svg}} [[Chechnya]]&nbsp;– Virtually independent from Russia from 1996 as '''[[Chechen Republic of Ichkeria]]''', however the country was recognized only by [[Taliban]]. After terrorist attacks in 1999 the republic was returned to Russia's control in the [[Second Chechen War]].
*{{Flagcountry|Confederate States of America|1861}}&nbsp;– Occupied the southeastern [[United States]], stretching from [[Texas]] to [[Virginia]]. Declared secession from the U.S. in 1861, reintegrated into the U.S. in 1865. [[Reconstruction era of the United States|Reconstruction]] ended in 1876 and U.S. troops withdrew as an occupation force in 1877. [[South Carolina]] was the first state to declare its secession from the United States, doing so on December 20, 1860. Political factions in the "border states" of [[Kentucky]] and [[Missouri]] declared themselves parts of the Confederacy and controlled small portions of those regions early in the war. The major Indian tribes in Oklahoma signed an alliance with the Confederacy, and participated in its military efforts against the U.S. 
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of the Crimean ASSR (1938).svg}} [[Crimean Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic]], declared independence from [[Ukraine]] in 1992 but soon settled for being an autonomous republic within Ukraine.
* [[Cruzob]], achieved independence from Mexico in 1856, but was reannexed in 1901.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Green Ukraine.svg}} [[Green Ukraine]]&nbsp;– Declared independence from [[Far Eastern Republic]] in 1920, dissolved in 1922.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of the Croatian Republic of Herzeg-Bosnia.svg}} [[Croatian Republic of Herzeg-Bosnia|Herzeg-Bosnia]]&nbsp;– Separated from [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]] in 1992, reincorporated into the country in 1994.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Italy (1946–2003).svg}} [[Italian Social Republic]] (1943–1945)
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Katanga.svg}} [[Katanga Province|Katanga]]&nbsp;– Declared its independence of the newly formed [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]] in 1960, was incorporated again into the country in 1963.
* [[File:Flag of Kosova (1991–1999).svg|23px|border]] [[Republic of Kosova|Kosova]] – Occupied [[Serbia and Montenegro|Yugoslav]] territory from 1991 until 1999.
* [[Republic of Manitoba|Manitoba]]&nbsp;– short-lived republic led by Thomas Spence, declared after the [[Hudson's Bay Company]] gave up [[Rupert's Land]] and before the government of Canada took control (1867).
*{{Flagicon image|Metis Blue.svg}} [[Red River Rebellion]]&nbsp;– provisional government in [[Rupert's Land]], led by [[Louis Riel]] in (1869–1870).
*{{Flagicon image|State Flag of Serbian Krajina (1991).svg}} [[Republic of Serbian Krajina|Serbian Krajina]]&nbsp;– declared independence from [[Croatia]] in 1991, reincorporated into the country in 1995.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of South Kasai.svg}} [[South Kasai]]&nbsp;– declared independence from the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]] in June 1960, reincorporated into the country in December 1961.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Russia-2.png|size=23px}} [[Supreme Administration of Northern Region]]&nbsp;– Proclaimed independent in 1918, later became the [[Provisional Government of the Northern Region]].
*{{Flagcountry|Republika Srpska (1992–95)}}&nbsp;– Separated from [[Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina|Bosnia and Herzegovina]] in 1992, reincorporated into the country in 1995.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of the Principality of Trinidad.svg}} [[Principality of Trinidad]]&nbsp;– Declared independence in 1893, claimed by United Kingdom in 1895, but incorporated by Brazil.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of the Autonomous Province of Western Bosnia (1993–1995).svg}} [[Autonomous Province of Western Bosnia|Western Bosnia]]&nbsp;– Declared independence from [[Republic of Bosnia and Herzegovina|Bosnia and Herzegovina]] in 1993 and reincorporated into it in 1995.

===Annexed countries===
These nations, once separate, are now part of another country. Cases of voluntary accession are included.
*{{Flagicon image|Labaro Reggenza Italiana del Carnaro.svg}} [[Italian Regency of Carnaro|Regency of Carnaro]] in 1919 and {{Flagicon image|Flag of the Free State of Fiume.svg}} [[Free State of Fiume]] 1920–1924, two short-lived states in the port city of [[Rijeka|Fiume/Rijeka]] proclaimed by [[Gabriele D'Annunzio]]. Following World War I, the city was disputed between Italy and [[Yugoslavia]], and eventually captured by Italy in 1921. The city passed to [[Yugoslavia]] after World War II and is now in Croatia.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of the Couto Misto.svg}} [[Couto Misto]] – Tiny 10th century border territory that was split between Spain and Portugal in 1864–8.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Cretan State.svg}} [[Cretan State|Crete]] – Autonomous under [[Ottoman Empire|Ottoman]] [[suzerainty]] in 1898, unilaterally declared union with Greece in 1908, which was recognized in 1913.
*{{Flag|East Germany}} – Annexed by [[West Germany]] in 1990 and now part of [[Germany]].
*{{Flagcountry|Kingdom of England}} – Merged with Scotland to form the Kingdom of Great Britain in 1706, now part of the United Kingdom.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Franceville.svg}} [[Franceville, New Hebrides|Franceville]] (1889-1890) – Independent in 1889, later governed by France and Britain as part of the [[New Hebrides]]; now part of [[Vanuatu]].
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Hatay.svg}} [[Republic of Hatay|Hatay]] – Part of the Mandate of Syria that became part of Turkey; independent 1938–1939
*{{Flagcountry|Kingdom of Hawaii}} – Annexed by the U.S. in the late 19th century.
*{{Flagicon image|KalatFlag.gif}} [[Kalat (princely state)|Kalat]] (1638,1666-1955) – 1666 to 1955, became part of [[Pakistan]].
* Free States of [[Menton]] and [[Roquebrune-Cap Martin|Roquebrune]] – Seceded from [[Monaco]] in 1848, under nominal protection of the [[Kingdom of Sardinia]], then annexed by France in 1861.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Moresnet.svg}} [[Moresnet]] – 1816–1920, Tiny European territory that endured for a hundred years before definitively becoming part of Belgium.
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of Natalia Republic.svg}} [[Natalia Republic]] – 1839–1843, Was quickly made into a British colony
*{{Flagicon image|Flag of the Islands of Refreshment.svg}} [[Islands of Refreshment]] – The islands of [[Tristan da Cunha]] were settled in 1810 and declared independence in 1811. Annexed by the United Kingdom in 1815.
*{{Flagcountry|Kingdom of Scotland|1542}} – Merged with England to form the Kingdom of Great Britain in 1706, now part of the United Kingdom.
* {{Flag|South Vietnam}} – Occupied by [[North Vietnam]] in 1975 and annexed into it in 1976.
*{{Flagicon image|Tatar Nationalist Flag.svg}} [[Republic of Tatarstan|Islamic Republic of Tatarstan]] – Existed from 1992 until annexed by [[Russia]] in 1994.
*{{Flagcountry|Transvaal}} – Now part of South Africa.
*{{Flagcountry|Republic of Texas}} – Annexed by the U.S. in 1845.
*{{Flagcountry|West Germany}} - Now part of Germany. Merged with [[East Germany]] in 1990.
