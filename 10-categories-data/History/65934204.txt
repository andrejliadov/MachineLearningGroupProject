{{short description|9th century Frankish administrative instrument}}
The '''Capitulary of Ver''' was issued by [[King Carloman]] in [[884]]. It is often known as the last [[Carolingian]] [[capitulary]]. It deals with issues including [[Viking]] attacks, the creation of [[guild]]s,<ref>{{Cite journal|last=Naismith|first=Rory|date=2020|title=Gilds, states and societies in the early Middle Ages|url=https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1111/emed.12433|journal=Early Medieval Europe|language=en|volume=28|issue=4|pages=627–662|doi=10.1111/emed.12433|issn=1468-0254}}</ref> the relation between kings and bishops,<ref>{{Citation|last=Bührer-Thierry|first=Geneviève|title=L'épiscopat en Francie orientale et occidentale à la fin du ixe siècle : Substitut ou soutien du pouvoir royal ?|date=2018-05-02|url=http://books.openedition.org/irhis/2580|work=La royauté et les élites dans l’Europe carolingienne (du début du ixe aux environs de 920)|pages=347–364|editor-last=Le Jan|editor-first=Régine|series=Histoire et littérature du Septentrion (IRHiS)|place=Lille|publisher=Publications de l’Institut de recherches historiques du Septentrion|isbn=978-2-905637-99-4|access-date=2020-11-25}}</ref> and the maintenance of peace.
