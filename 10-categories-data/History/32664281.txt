The '''World History Association Bentley Book Prize''' is an annual [[literary award]] given by the [[World History Association]].  It was first awarded in 1999 as the '''World History Association Book Prize'''; the name was changed in 2012 to honor [[Jerry H. Bentley]].<ref name="wha-bentley">{{cite web | url=http://www.thewha.org/wha-awards/wha-bentley-book-prize/ | title=WHA Bentley Book Prize | publisher=[[World History Association]] | accessdate=2 December 2015 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906124253/http://www.thewha.org/wha-awards/wha-bentley-book-prize/ | archive-date=6 September 2017 | url-status=dead }}</ref><ref name=home>[http://www.thewha.org/wha-awards/wha-bentley-book-prize/ World History Association Book Prize] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170906124253/http://www.thewha.org/wha-awards/wha-bentley-book-prize/ |date=6 September 2017 }}, official website.</ref><ref>Patrick Manning. ''Navigating world history: historians create a global past''. Palgrave Macmillan, 2003. [https://books.google.com/books?id=OGJUiUvTWAAC&pg=PA85 Pg. 85]</ref> The prize is $500. 

It should not be confused with the [[Jerry Bentley Prize in World History]], a similar book prize established in 2014 by the [[American Historical Association]].<ref>{{cite web | url=http://www.historians.org/awards-and-grants/awards-and-prizes/jerry-bentley-prize | title=Jerry Bentley Prize | publisher=[[American Historical Association]] | accessdate=4 September 2014}}</ref>
