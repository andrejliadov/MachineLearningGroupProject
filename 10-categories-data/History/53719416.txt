== [[2000s (decade)|2000s]] ==
<!--The 21st century began in 2001, not 2000 -->
===[[2001]]===
[[File:Dean kamen.jpg|[[Segway]] was unveiled in December 2001|thumb]]
*[[George W. Bush]] becomes [[President of the United States]].
*[[September 11 attacks]]: [[Al-Qaeda]] terrorists crash planes into the twin towers of the [[World Trade Center (1973–2001)|World Trade Center]] in [[New York City]], and [[the Pentagon]] in [[Washington, DC]]. A fourth plane is downed on the outskirts of [[Stonycreek Township, Somerset County, Pennsylvania|Stonycreek Township, Pennsylvania]]; 2,996 people die in the attacks.
*An [[2001 Gujarat earthquake|earthquake]] strikes [[Gujarat]], [[India]], on [[Republic Day (India)|Republic Day]], resulting in more than 20,000 deaths.
*The [[War on Terror]] is declared by [[George W. Bush|President Bush]], in response to [[September 11 attacks|9/11]].
*The media magnate [[Silvio Berlusconi]] is elected [[Prime Minister of Italy]].
*The [[United States invasion of Afghanistan|United States invades Afghanistan]] and topples the [[Islamic Emirate of Afghanistan|Taliban regime]], resulting in a [[War in Afghanistan (2001–present)|long-term war]].
*The [[al-Aqsa Intifada]] continues.
*The [[African Union]] is founded in [[Addis Ababa]], [[Ethiopia]].
*During an [[Argentine economic crisis (1999–2002)|economic crisis in Argentina]], the government effectively [[Corralito|freezes all bank accounts]] for twelve months which [[December 2001 riots in Argentina|leads to riots]] and President [[Fernando de la Rúa|de la Rúa]]'s resignation from office. There are five "presidents" in less than a month.
*President [[Joseph Estrada]] of the [[Philippines]] is [[Second EDSA Revolution|ousted]].
*[[Steve Jobs]] introduces the first [[iPod]].
*[[Dean Kamen]] unveils [[Segway]].
*[[China and the World Trade Organization|China becomes]] a member of the [[World Trade Organization]].
*[[Wikipedia]] is launched.
*Eleven members of the royal family of [[Nepal]], including the king and queen, [[Nepalese royal massacre|are killed]] by Crown Prince [[Dipendra of Nepal|Dipendra]], who wounds himself and dies three days later.

===[[2018]]===
*[[Turkey]] [[Operation Olive Branch|invades northern Syria]], while 70 die in [[Douma chemical attack|a chemical attack]], triggering a [[2018 missile strikes against Syria|missile strike]] against [[Bashar al-Assad]].
*The Trump administration reimposes [[sanctions against Iran]]. 
*The first monkeys are [[Cloning|cloned]], and first genetically modified humans reported, in China.
*[[Jacob Zuma]] resigns as [[President of South Africa]]; [[Cyril Ramaphosa]] becomes President. 
*[[Scott Morrison]] becomes [[Prime Minister of Australia]].
*Four people are poisoned, one to death, in [[Poisoning of Sergei and Yulia Skripal|Salisbury]] and [[2018 Amesbury poisonings|Amesbury]], England in a suspected Russian assassination attempt. The British government responds by leading the international expulsion of 153 Russian diplomats.
*[[March for Our Lives]] occurs in 900 locations worldwide in response to the [[Stoneman Douglas High School shooting|Parkland shooting]].
*The [[northern white rhinoceros]] becomes [[functionally extinct]].
*The university professor [[Giuseppe Conte]] becomes [[Prime Minister of Italy]] at the head of a populist coalition.
*[[Pakatan Harapan]] becomes the first opposition party to assume power in [[Malaysia]] since independence.
*The first summit between [[2018 North Korea–United States summit|the US and North Korea]] and the first ever crossing of the [[Korean Demilitarized Zone]] by a North Korean leader occur.
*Twenty-year [[Eritrean–Ethiopian border conflict]] formally ends.
*[[Yellow vests movement]] becomes [[France]]'s largest sustained period of civil unrest since 1968.
*The [[2018 Sunda Strait tsunami|Sunda strait tsunami]] kills 426 and injures 14,000 and the [[2018 Sulawesi earthquake and tsunami]] kills 4,340 and injures 10,700.
*Exiled ''[[Washington Post]]'' journalist [[Jamal Khashoggi]] is [[Assassination of Jamal Khashoggi|assassinated]] inside the Saudi consulate in Istanbul, triggering a [[Reactions to the assassination of Jamal Khashoggi|diplomatic crisis]] for Saudi Arabia.
*[[Kerch Strait incident]] triggers martial law in [[Ukraine]].
*[[Republic of Macedonia|Macedonia]] and [[Greece]] reach a historic agreement in the [[Macedonia naming dispute]], in which the former is renamed in 2019 to the 'Republic of North Macedonia'.
*China's [[National People's Congress]] votes to abolish presidential [[term limits]], allowing [[Xi Jinping]] to [[President for life|rule for life]]. Xi is also the [[General Secretary of the Communist Party of China]], the [[Paramount leader|highest position]] without term limits.
*[[Wedding of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle]].
*[[2018 Armenian revolution]] occurs.
*[[2018 Iraqi parliamentary election|First post-ISIS election in Iraq]].
*Twelve boys and their football coach are successfully [[Tham Luang cave rescue|rescued]] from the flooded [[Tham Luang Nang Non]] cave in [[Thailand]].
*[[France]] wins the [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] in Russia.
*[[First World War centenary|Leaders from more than 40 international organizations and dignitaries from nearly 200 countries attends the centennial of the ending of the First World War]].
*[[Bill Cosby sexual assault cases|Bill Cosby found guilty of drugging and sexually assaulting a woman and is sentenced to three to ten years in prison]].
*[[MIT EAD Airframe Version 2]] [[ion-propelled aircraft]] launched.

===[[2019]]===
*[[Jair Bolsonaro]] becomes [[President of Brazil]].
*[[New Horizons]] takes the first close up image of a [[486958 Arrokoth|classical kuiper belt object]].
*[[Chang'e 4]] becomes the first object to land on the [[far side of the Moon]].
*[[2019—20 Bolivian protests|Protests erupt in Bolivia]] [[2019 Venezuelan presidential crisis|and Venezuela]] over [[2018 Venezuelan presidential election|disputed]] [[2019 Bolivian general election|elections]].
*[[Nursultan Nazarbayev]] resigns as [[President of Kazakhstan]]. [[Kassym-Jomart Tokayev]] assumes power. [[Astana]] is renamed [[Nur-Sultan]] in his honor.
*[[Christchurch mosque shootings]] kill 51 people, while a [[2019 Khash–Zahedan suicide bombing|suicide bombing in Iran]] kills 41, and a [[2019 Sri Lanka Easter bombings|series of bomb attacks]] in [[Sri Lanka]] kills 250.
*The [[Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant]] loses the last of its territory.
*The EU [[Directive on Copyright in the Digital Single Market]] is passed over intense opposition.
*[[Abdelaziz Bouteflika]] resigns as [[President of Algeria]], while [[Omar al-Bashir]] [[2019 Sudanese coup d'état|is deposed]] as [[President of Sudan]] in [[2019 Sudanese coup d'état|a coup d'état]] amid [[2019 Algerian protests|widespread protests]] [[2018–19 Sudanese protests|in both countries]].
*The [[Event Horizon Telescope]] takes the first ever image of a [[black hole]], at the core of galaxy [[Messier 87]].
*[[Volodymyr Zelensky]] becomes [[President of Ukraine]].
*[[Victor Vescovo]] breaks the human depth record, reaching 10,928&nbsp;m in the [[Challenger Deep]].
*[[Akihito]] [[2019 Japanese imperial transition|abdicates]] as [[Emperor of Japan]]; [[Naruhito]] becomes Emperor.
*[[Bashar al Assad]] launches [[Northwestern Syria offensive (April–August 2019)|multiple offensives]] [[Northwestern Syria offensive (November 2019–present)|in Northwestern Syria]]; [[Turkey]] launches [[2019 Turkish offensive into north-eastern Syria|an offensive into northeastern Syria]].
*[[Khartoum massacre|More than a hundred people are killed]] after police and [[Janjaweed]] attack protesters in Sudan.
*The United States blames [[June 2019 Gulf of Oman incident|attacks on ships in the Gulf of Oman]] on Iran, escalating [[2019–20 Persian Gulf crisis|tensions in the Persian Gulf]].
*[[Kyriakos Mitsotakis]] becomes [[Prime Minister of Greece]].
*[[Boris Johnson]] becomes [[Prime Minister of the United Kingdom]], and attempts a [[2019 British prorogation controversy|prorogation of Parliament]] that is [[R (Miller) v The Prime Minister and Cherry v Advocate General for Scotland|ultimately declared unlawful]].
*[[2019–20 Hong Kong protests|Protests begin]] in [[Hong Kong]] over an extradition bill.
*[[India]] [[Revocation of the special status of Jammu and Kashmir|revokes the special status of Jammu and Kashmir]].
*[[Barisha raid]] ends in the [[Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi|death of Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi]].
*[[2019 Amazon rainforest wildfires|Wildfires spike]] in [[Brazil]], while [[Australia]] endures the [[2019–20 Australian bushfire season|most widespread brush fires in its history]].
*A major [[Notre-Dame de Paris fire|fire]] engulfs [[Notre-Dame de Paris|Notre-Dame Cathedral]] in Paris, resulting in the roof and main spire collapsing.
*An [[2019 Philippines measles outbreak|outbreak of measles]] and [[2019–2020 Philippines polio outbreak|polio]] is declared in the Philippines, 19 years after polios eradication in the country.
*Fossil fragments found in the [[Callao Cave]] in the [[Philippines]] reveal the existence of the ''[[Homo luzonensis]]'' species of humans.
*The [[2019–2020 dengue fever epidemic]] begins in [[Southeast Asia]].
*[[2019 Davao del Sur earthquake|A 6.9 magnitude earthquake]] in [[Davao del Sur]], kills 13 people, and [[Typhoon Phanfone]] kills 50 people and causes $60 billion in damages, in the [[Philippines]].
*The [[United States Department of Justice|U.S. Justice Department]] charges Chinese tech firm [[Huawei]] with multiple counts of fraud.
*[[WikiLeaks]] co-founder [[Julian Assange]] is [[Indictment and arrest of Julian Assange|arrested]] in London.
*''[[Avengers: Endgame]]'' was released in theaters, breaking many box-office records, including becoming the [[List of highest-grossing films|highest-grossing movies of all time]].
*Isabelle Holdaway is the first patient to receive a genetically modified [[phage therapy]] to treat a [[Antimicrobial resistance|drug-resistant infection]].
*More than 50 prisoners are [[Amazonas prison massacres|killed in a series of riots]] in [[Amazonas (Brazilian state)|Amazonas]], Brazil.
*[[2019 Koreas–United States DMZ Summit|A trilateral gathering was held at the Panmunjom Truce Village between South Korean President Moon Jae-in, North Korean Leader Kim Jong-un and United States President Donald Trump]].
*[[Ursula von der Leyen]] becomes [[President of the European Commission]].
*[[United States v. Guzmán|Joaquín "El Chapo" Guzmán found guilty of drug trafficking, money laundering and murder and is sentenced to 30 years in prison]].
*[[2019–2020 Japan–South Korea trade dispute|Japan and South Korea trade dispute]].
*[[Death of Sahar Khodayari|Sahar Khodayari]] dies after setting herself on fire after being arrested for attending a soccer game in [[Iran]].
*[[Trial of Catalonia independence leaders|Twelve Catalan independence movement leaders found guilty of sedition and misuse of public funds and they were sentenced to 9 to 13 years in prison]].
*[[NASA]] astronauts [[Jessica Meir]] and [[Christina Koch]] conducts the first all-female spacewalk outside of the [[International Space Station|ISS]].
*Israeli Prime Minister [[Benjamin Netanyahu]] is indicted on charges of bribery, fraud and breach of trust.
*[[Sanna Marin]] becomes [[Prime Minister of Finland]].
*[[Pope Francis]] abolishes [[Pontifical secret|pontifical secrecy]] in [[Catholic Church sexual abuse cases|sex abuse cases]].
*[[Greta Thunberg]] delivers "How dare you" speech at the [[2019 UN Climate Action Summit]].
*The [[President of the United States]], [[Donald Trump]], is [[Impeachment of Donald Trump|impeached by the House of Representatives]] but [[Impeachment trial of Donald Trump|acquitted by the Senate]].
*The [[COVID-19 pandemic]] begins in [[Wuhan, China]]; the start of an ongoing global [[pandemic]].
