== Births ==
*[[Averroes]] (1126&ndash;1198), [[Al-Andalus|Andalusian]] polymath.
*[[Zhu Xi]] (1130&ndash;1200), [[Neo-Confucianism|Neo-Confucian]] philosopher, politician, and writer of the [[Song dynasty]].
