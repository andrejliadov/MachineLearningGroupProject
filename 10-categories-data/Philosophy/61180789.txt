'''Ashok Gangadean''' (born September 26, 1941) is a [[Trinidadian]] [[philosopher]], author and spiritual activist. He is the Margaret Gest Professor of Global Philosophy at [[Haverford College]] in [[Haverford, Pennsylvania|Haverford]], [[Pennsylvania]] and the Founder and Director of the Global Dialogue Institute.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.haverford.edu/users/agangade|title=Ashok Gangadean {{!}} Haverford College|website=www.haverford.edu|language=en|access-date=2019-06-30}}</ref> He was the first Director of the Margaret Gest Center for the Cross-Cultural Study of Religion at Haverford and is Co-Convenor of the recently formed World Commission on Global Consciousness and Spirituality.<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://lifeboat.com/ex/bios.ashok.k.gangadean|title=Lifeboat Foundation Bios: Dr. Ashok K. Gangadean|website=lifeboat.com|access-date=2019-06-30}}</ref>
