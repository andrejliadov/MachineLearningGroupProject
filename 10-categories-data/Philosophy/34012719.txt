In [[Christian theology]], a '''supernatural gift''' is a gift of [[God in Christianity|God]] that is conferred on [[Human|man]] that is above all the powers of created nature.<reF>{{CathEncy|wstitle=Supernatural Gift}}</ref> An example of this is the gift of [[Grace in Christianity|grace]] which enables man to attain salvation despite [[original sin]].
