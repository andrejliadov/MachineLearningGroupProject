'''''Projet de communauté philosophe''''' (translated as '''''Project for a Philosophical Community''''') is a book written by the [[French people|French]] philosopher [[Victor d'Hupay]], published in 1777.

== Review == 
This book can be seen as a cornerstone in the history of [[communism]], as it describes for the first time how "communists" (people living in a "[[commune]]") should understand this philosophy.{{Dubious|reason=Not consistent with RS. See discussion at d'Hupay's talk page.|date=July 2019}} 
In this work, the author advises to "share all economic and material products between inhabitants of the 'commune', so that all may benefit from everybody's work".<ref name="Jean-Pierre Cassely, 2016">Cassely, 2016 : ''Aix insolite et secrète'' JonGlez {{pp.|192-193}} ([http://catalogue.bnf.fr/ark:/12148/cb45058387v references from the Bibliothèque nationale de France])</ref> Later, in a 1785 letter to friend [[Restif de la Bretonne]], d'Hupay would describe himself as a "communist" author.<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.hupay.fr/ |title=Archived copy |accessdate=2017-03-08 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170131191812/http://www.hupay.fr/ |archivedate=2017-01-31 }}</ref><ref name="Grandjonc Jacques, 1983">Grandjonc Jacques,1983: ''Quelques dates à propos des termes communiste et communisme'' In: Mots, n°7, Octobre 1983 {{p.|146}} ([http://www.persee.fr/doc/mots_0243-6450_1983_num_7_1_1122 reference on the Persée library])</ref>

The book was partly written in d'Hupay's own [[bastide]] in [[Meyreuil]], near [[Aix-en-Provence]], where he was determined to set his own commune and live by the communist philosophy, with neighbours and friends.<ref>{{Cite web |url=http://www.hupay.fr/pages/vie-d-hupay.html |title=Archived copy |access-date=2017-01-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170202024755/http://www.hupay.fr/pages/vie-d-hupay.html |archive-date=2017-02-02 |url-status=dead }}</ref>
