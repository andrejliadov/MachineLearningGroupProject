'''''Closer to Truth''''' is a [[television series]] on public television<ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.wgbh.org/programs/Closer-to-Truth-14 |title=Closer to Truth |publisher=Wgbh.org |date= |accessdate=2014-06-17 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131203005852/http://www.wgbh.org/programs/Closer-to-Truth-14 |archive-date=2013-12-03 |url-status=dead }}</ref><ref>{{cite web |url=http://www.mptv.org/schedule/a-z/program/closer-to-truth/ |title=Closer to Truth schedule on MPTV |publisher=Mptv.org |accessdate=2014-06-17 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141027164202/http://www.mptv.org/schedule/a-z/program/closer-to-truth/ |archivedate=2014-10-27 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.cpt12.org/tv_schedule/program_details.cfm?id=220130812220000 |title=Colorado Public Television, Denver PBS, CPT12 (Formerly KBDI) |publisher=Cpt12.org |accessdate=2014-06-17 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131202224330/http://www.cpt12.org/tv_schedule/program_details.cfm?id=220130812220000 |archivedate=2013-12-02 }}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://apthd.com/series/1650/Closer-to-Truth/ |title=Closer to Truth on Alabama Public Television |publisher=Apthd.com |accessdate=2014-06-17 |url-status=dead |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20140806160151/http://www.apthd.com/series/1650/Closer-to-Truth/ |archivedate=2014-08-06 }}</ref> originally created, produced and hosted by [[Robert Lawrence Kuhn]]. The original series aired in 2000 for two seasons, followed by a second series aired in 2003 for a single season. The third series of the program, '''''Closer to Truth: Cosmos. Consciousness. God''''', launched in 2008, with 19 full seasons to date. ''Closer to Truth'' airs on over 200 [[PBS]] and public television stations and has had over 200,000 station broadcasts.

The show is centered on on-camera conversations with leading scientists, philosophers, theologians, and scholars, covering a diverse range of topics or questions, from the cause, size and nature of the universe (or [[multiverse]]), to the mystery of consciousness and the notion of free will, to the existence and essence of God, to the mystery of existence (i.e., why there is anything at all).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.kqed.org/tv/programs/index.jsp?pgmid=17540 |title=Closer to Truth &#124; KQED Public Media for Northern CA |publisher=Kqed.org |date= |accessdate=2014-06-17}}</ref><ref name="blogspot1">{{cite web|url=http://scienceandreligiontoday.blogspot.com/2008/09/were-getting-closer-to-truth.html |title=Science & Religion Today: We're Getting "Closer to Truth" |publisher=Scienceandreligiontoday.blogspot.com |date=2008-09-10 |accessdate=2014-06-17}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|author=Luke Muehlhauser |url=http://commonsenseatheism.com/?p=1977 |title=Closer to Truth |publisher=Commonsenseatheism.com |date=2009-06-05 |accessdate=2014-06-17}}</ref>

The ''Closer to Truth'' website features extensive conversations in addition to those that have been broadcast on TV (approximately 4,000 videos).<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.closertotruth.com/about/content-guide |title=Content Guide |publisher=Closer to Truth |date= |accessdate=2014-06-17}}</ref> It is the world's largest archive of video interviews with leading experts in the philosophy of cosmology and physics, consciousness, and the philosophy of religion.

Robert Lawrence Kuhn is the creator, executive producer, writer and presenter of the series.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.choprafoundation.org/speakers/robert-kuhn/ |title=Robert Lawrence Kuhn « The Chopra Foundation |publisher=Choprafoundation.org |date= |accessdate=2014-06-17}}</ref> Peter Getzels is the co-creator, producer and director.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.bethesdamagazine.com/Bethesda-Magazine/September-October-2013/Peter-Getzels/ |title=Reel Life - Bethesda Magazine - September-October 2013 - Bethesda, MD |publisher=Bethesda Magazine |date= |accessdate=2014-06-17}}</ref><ref>https://www.imdb.com/name/nm2011631/</ref>
